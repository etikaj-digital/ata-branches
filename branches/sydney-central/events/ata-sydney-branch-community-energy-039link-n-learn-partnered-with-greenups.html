ATA Sydney branch - Community Energy \&#039;link n learn’ (partnered with GreenUps)

  • 2015-11-03 06:00:00
  • 107 Redfern Street, Redfern, 2016

When: Tuesday November 3rd, 5-10pm (tours from 5, talks at 7)

Where: \'107 Projects\', 107 Redfern Street, Redfern, 2016 (click here for a map)

Cost: $10 early bird tickets on Eventbrite, $15 on the door. Ticket includes entry into the Prize Draw.

The ATA Sydney branch have teamed up with GreenUps to present this great event on Communty Energy (along with Ethinvest, C4CE and Community Power Agency).

For the November Greenups, we’ve invited a range of Community Energy organisations to come and share their ideas and projects, all to to inspire, inform and allow you to get on board.
With 87 per cent of Australians supporting renewable energy from rooftop solar, and a solar system sold every three minutes, it comes as no surprise that community groups across the country are increasingly taking matters into their own hands and pursuing local, community-owned energy projects.
These community-led projects allow local groups to take control of their own energy future, reduce their carbon footprint, and reinvest their energy bill savings back into the community for its social needs.
Come along to hear first hand from the these groups, what they\'ve achieved, what they have planned, and how you can get involved.

VENUE:
107 Projects, Redfern. 107 Projects is multidisciplinary community creative space run by a team of artists and passionate creatives as a vanguard for public engagement and social enrichment through the arts. They are loyal supporters of Greenups and what it stands for.
FOOD:
The Veggie Patch Van will be outside offering tasty vegetarian food. The Veggie Patch is devoted to providing high-quality vegetarian food using seasonal produce sourced from local growers.
DOOR PRIZES:
  • 1 x $500 Contribution to a community energy project of your choice. Care of Greenups and ATA.
  • 2 x 1 year subscription to Sanctuary Magazine. thanks to ATA.
  • 1 x Financial consultation with a financial planner. thanks to Ethinvest
TICKETS:
$10 early bird tickets on Eventbrite, $15 on the door. Ticket includes entry into the Prize Draw.
GreenUps is run as a not-for-profit and charge a small fee to cover the costs of putting the events on and to place a value on (and therefore create a commitment to) the act of RSVPing.
All funds raised this month will go to funding the amazing door-prizes, and to cover the cost of room hire and public liability insurance.

So come along, exchange creative juices, tall stories and radical strategies for how we can repair our broken world. Be inspired by short sharp talks & mingle with folks immersed-in or curious about making what they do more sustainable.

Hopefully see you there!
