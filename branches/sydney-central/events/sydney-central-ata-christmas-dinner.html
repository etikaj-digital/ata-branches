Sydney Central ATA Christmas Dinner

  • 2014-12-02 07:30:00

When: Tuesday 2 December, 6:30pm \'til not too late

Where: Private Residence: 73 Edward St. Darlington (must register via Eventbrite to attend)

Celebrate the festive season with fellow members of the ATA and Australian Solar Council and good cheer with free pizza and sparkling conversation to end a successful year!

We are at a time when solar power is reframing the global energy supply and offering tantalising opportunities of a more equitable and democratic energy supply. This is a chance to chat with like-minded people from both groups who continue to change the world, advance sustainability and find the path to world peace over a few drinks and pizza.

Other Details:

  • Event is BYO
  • We will have made some tasty accompaniments but you are welcome to bring extra food such as salads
  • Donations towards the branch are always gratefully accepted

Transport info.
73 Edward St. is a five minute walk from Redfern Station - map

  1. On exiting the station Head west (turn left) toward Little Eveleigh St (40m)
  2. Continue on Lawson St for approx. 200m
  3. At the intersection with Abercrombie St., cross the street to find Ivy Lane, diagaonally across from the Glengarry Hotel
  4. Walk down Ivy Lane passing Edward Lane and turn right onto Edward St.

Register by selecting ATTEND @ atasydney.eventbrite.com.au
