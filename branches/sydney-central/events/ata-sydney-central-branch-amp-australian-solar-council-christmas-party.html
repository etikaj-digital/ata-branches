ATA Sydney Central Branch &amp; Australian Solar Council Christmas Party

  • 2013-12-10 18:30:00
  • 565 George Serviceway, Sydney, NSW, Australia
The ATA Sydney Central Branch & Australian Solar Council wish to invite you to come to their Christmas Party on Tuesday, the 10th of December from 6.30-9.00pm in the Glass Room, a private area within Joe\'s Kitchen Restaurant and Bar on Level 2 of the City of Sydney RSL Club, 565 George St (the corner of Liverpool St). Click here for a map or here for details on the venue.
 
It will be a great chance to mull over the year’s events and chat with like-minded people from both groups who continue to change the world, advance sustainability and can find the path to world peace over a few drinks and nice food. The location is very close to public transport and the prices are reasonable.
All are invited to attend and we look forward to seeing you there.
Please Register by selecting ATTEND @ atasydney.eventbrite.com.au

Are you thinking of a more sustainable Christmas?

  • Consider supporting ATA\'s international projects:  http://shop.ata.org.au/shop/donate-to-ata-itp
  • Or use this excellent guide put together by Milkwood Permaculture:  http://milkwood.net/2013/12/03/20-gift-ideas-for-a-planet-friendly-christmas/

Thanks again for your involvement with and support of Sydney Central ATA over what has been a great year where we have covered everything from phase change materials to straw bale building and battery storage to window glazing.  Our next meeting will not be until 11th February 2014 and be assured that we have an exciting array of speakers lined up for 2014.

The ATA Sydney Central Committee wish you all the best for Christmas and a prosperous and renewable new year!
