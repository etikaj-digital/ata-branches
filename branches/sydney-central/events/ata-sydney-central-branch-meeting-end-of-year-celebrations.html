ATA Sydney Central branch meeting - End of Year Celebrations

  • 2015-12-08 07:30:00
  • 73 Edward St, Darlington NSW 2008

When: Tuesday 8 December 2015, 6:30pm - 8:30pm
Where: 73 Edward St, Darlington NSW 2008 (about 400m from Redfern Station). Click here for a map.
What to bring: BYO Drinks

To celebrate a great year for ATA Sydney Central and Australian Solar Council Sydney Chapter we will be gathering at David\'s home in Darlington. This is a great opportunity to catch up and chat with the ATA/ASC team and other members in a relaxed setting.  There will be a selection of delicious pizzas but drinks are BYO.

Other Events:

ASC End of Year Networking Drinks, Wednesday 2 December 2015, 5:00pm - 6:40pm, Rydges Sydney Central, 28 Albion Street, Surry Hills. This will follow on from an informative energy storage training session in the afternoon that starts at 3pm. Click here to purchase tickets and to get more information. Members - $33.00 inc GST; Non-members - $66.00 inc GST.

We look forward to seeing you at one of these great events.  Thanks again for your support and the committee wish you a very merry Christmas and a solar-powered and sustainable new year!
