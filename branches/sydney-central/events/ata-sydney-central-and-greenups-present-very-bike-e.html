ATA Sydney Central and Greenups present Very Bike-E!

  • 2014-10-07 08:00:00
  • 80 George St. Redfern NSW
Event Name: Very Bike-E! - October GreenUps explores electric bicycles in collaboration with ATA Sydney Central branch
Date & Time: Tuesday 7 October from 6:00-10pm

Location: Work-Shop, 80 George St. Redfern
Cost: $11.59 (register through EventBrite link below..)

ATA Sydney Central has teamed up with GreenUps to see what happens when you add some electricity to your pedal power.  We will be at WORK-SHOP a warehouse of maker goodness located at 80 George St, Redfern, alongside the cycleway.  There is Bike Valet Parking upon arrival and you can get a tune-up from the crew at The Rolling Fix.  On the night you can ride all kinds of E-Bikes on offer from Sydney Electric Bikes and Glow Worm Bicycles.  Create your own electricity on Milk Crate\'s pedal powered sound system...but don\'t over do it or your favourite tune will be out of sync!  Expect inspiring speakers, delicious eats, the stylish folks from trolleyd offering up a tasty selection of drinks and the usual great GreenUps crowd.
