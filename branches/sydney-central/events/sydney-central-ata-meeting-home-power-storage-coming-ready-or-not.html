Sydney Central ATA Meeting - Home Power Storage - Coming Ready or Not!

  • 2013-11-12 18:00:00
  • Wallaby Room, 11th Floor, Telstra Building, 320 Pitt St Sydney

Home Power Storage - Coming Ready or Not!

Speaker Bio:  Blair Pester is the founder of WINAICO Australia Pty Ltd, a high quality PV manufacturer distributing directly within Australia, and a subsidiary of WIN WIN Precision Technology in Taiwan.Blair’s previous work in electronics engineering has seen him work on efficiency and accuracy in state of the art electronic devices.  His experience in the semiconductor field led him to work in the Solar PV industry.  He has chosen to work with companies that innovate in the production of high quality solar products.  Colin Dedman in his popular PV testing  feature in ReNew Issue 124 described that "the newly tested Winaico polycrystalline panels set the proverbial cat among the pigeons, with a record-breaking yield performance of 108.8% under winter conditions."
For more information about WINAICO go to:

Talk summary:  Home energy storage has the potential to decentralise our energy systems and to further improve the economics of and utility of Solar PV.  The talk will focus on:

- types of technologies available in terms of battery storage

- regulatory requirements - what do you need to know to install battery storage?

- effectively pairing battery storage with solar PV output
