Welcome to the new ‘Tas South’ branch of the ATA.

We are working in conjunction with the local ASC chapter and interest has been good. We meet at the Duke of Wellington Hotel, 192 Macquarie St, Hobart. Please see details below for our activities or check the calendar.

Convenor: Rick Rolls

Secretary: Nigel Legge

Check out the Passive Design for Cool Temperate Climates information sheets.

If you’re a local ATA member then join us and show your enthusiasm for sustainable living!
