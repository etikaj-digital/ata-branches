ATA Tas South branch - General meeting - committee elections

  • 2017-02-06 06:45:00
  • 192 Macquarie St, Hobart TAS

When: Monday 6 February 2017, 5.45pm before the ASC meeting at 6.15 pm

Where: Duke of Wellington Hotel, 192 Macquarie St. Hobart

A meeting to vote for the Tas South ATA branch committee at 5.45 pm before the Australian Solar Council meeting. This is a crucial initial step in establishing a local branch and needs your support.

Currently the plan is that the new ATA Tas South branch will operate in cooperation with the ASC events and help to broaden the range of topics and reach of the meetings. A number of people have indicate their willingness to be on the committee to hep organise branch activities. Come along and encourage this new initiative and meet some of your fellow ATA members. All are welcome, but only ATA members are able to vote for the committee.

Any questions: Doug, ATA branch coordinator (doug@ata.org.au)
