ATA Tas South branch meeting - Prefabricated flat pack panels for new houses and extensions

  • 2018-02-05 08:30:00
  • 192 Macquarie Street, Hobart, TAS

When: Monday 5 February 2017, 7.30 pm
Where: Function Room (upstairs), The Duke of Welllington Hotel, 192 Macquarie Street, Hobart
Cost:Gold coin donation

Presentation:

Warren French, architect and manager of Tasmanian flat-pack company Valley Workshop, and Uta Green, architect from Green Design, will discuss structurally insulated flat pack panels (SIP\'s) and their use at a new bushwalkers hut at Lake Tahune on the Frenchman\'s Cap walking track.

The meeting will be preceded by the ASC/ATA committee meeting at 6.15 pm.

All ATA members and the general public are invited warmly invited and we look forward to seeing you at the meeting.

Nigel Legge, Secretary
