ATA Tas South Branch meeting - Electric vehicles

  • 2018-09-03 09:30:00
  • 192 Macquarie Street, Hobart, TAS

When: Monday 3 September 2018, 6:15pm General meeting, Guest speaker at 7.30 pm
Where: Function Room (upstairs), The Duke of Welllington Hotel, 192 Macquarie Street, Hobart
Cost: Gold coin donation

Presentation:  Michael Wadsley, the Vice-Chair of the Tasmanian Branch of the Australian Electric Vehicle Association will speak about recent and future changes to electric vehicles, including the increase in battery size and range, and will also discuss moves to produce electric boats, trucks and planes. The talk will also cover successful examples of conversion of older conventional vehicles to electric drive.

We hope to see you at the meeting. All welcome.
