ATA Tas South branch meeting - Three Sustainable House Day houses

  • 2017-09-04 09:30:00
  • 192 Macquarie Street, Hobart, TAS

When: Monday 4 Sept 2017, 7.30 pm
Where: The Duke of Welllington Hotel, 192 Macquarie Street, Hobart
Cost: Free

Presentation: \'Three Sustainable House Day houses\'

Designers and builders of three of the Sustainable House Day houses that will be open on the 17th September, will give a short presentation on each of their houses, and will answer questions from the floor.

If you are a future sustainable home builder or renovator, and are interested in visiting the houses, this talk will provide a detailed insight into the challenges involved and successes achieved in the design and construction of the houses, and a taster of what will be available on the day.

We look forward to seeing you at the meeting.

Nigel Legge, Secretary
