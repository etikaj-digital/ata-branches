ATA Wollongong branch meeting - Future electricity demands and distribution

  • 2017-03-02 07:00:00
  • Northfields Ave, Wollongong NSW 2522

When: Thursday 2 March 2017, 6:00pm for refreshments and a chat - then talks start at 6:30pm

Where: Room 122, Building 40, University of Wollongong, Northfields Ave, Wollongong NSW 2522 (same building and room as last time). Click here for a campus map and here for a Google Map.

Cost: A gold coin donation to cover refreshments would be appreciated.

Presentations:

Tim Randall (Randall Organic) and Doug Paine (Ex NSW Grid Controller) will talk about future electricity demands and distribution.
Retail your own solar energy to your neighbour? Earn money charging other peoples cars? Or store it up with your own battery? The possibilities are endless. How will you manage your mirco-grid in the future?
