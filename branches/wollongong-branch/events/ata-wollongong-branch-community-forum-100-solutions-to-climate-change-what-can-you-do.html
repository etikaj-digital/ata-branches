ATA Wollongong branch - Community Forum - 100 solutions to Climate Change: What can you do?

  • 2017-10-12 07:30:00
  • Innovation Way, North Wollongong, NSW
When: Thursday 12 October 2017, 6:30pm for 7:00pm start

Where: UOW Innovation Campus (Squires Way, North Wollongong) - ITAMS Building, building 233, room G01.

Click here for a Google Map and here for a campus map. Parking is free from 6:30pm in P1 (near Science Centre) or P5 (south of Building 233)

Cost: A gold coin donation to cover refreshments would be appreciated.

Forum Outline:
Paul Hawken\'s Drawdown (2017) gives 100 solutions to attack climate change. The Alternative Technology Association (ATA),Wollongong Branch, is hosting a forum on the ideas in Drawdown that relate to people living in the Illawarra.

Speakers involved in Sustainable House Days, Architectural Design and Electric Cars will cover relevant topics and stimulate questions and discussion.

  • Tom Hunt - Drawdown summary
  • Dion Zappacosta - Towards SHD 2018 with passive design and insulation
  • Neville Lockhart - Solar PV, batteries, heat pumps, LEDs
  • Tom Hunt - Solar hot water
  • Judy Lockhart - Reflective & honeycomb blinds, window films, ventilation
  • Walter Koeler  - Electric cars, batteries and PV
