James Cook University Sustainability Symposium and fair

  • 2014-04-11 09:00:00
  • James Cook University , Smithfield campus

Symposium runs from 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM and the fair runs in conjunction from 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM . Venue is the James Cook University campus at Smithfield in Cairns. This Year\'s theme is "Challenging Consumerism : The War on Waste".
ATA Cairns has been invited to display at the Fair. We will be having a stall to show off our new Canopy and displaying several models, including a very innovative way of dealing with the waste pulp from juicing machines.
