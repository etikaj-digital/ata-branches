Safe Climate, Clean Energy: How do we move to renewable energy powering Queensland?

  • 2016-08-24 08:00:00
  • 288 Edward St, Brisbane, 4000

When: Wednesday 24 August 2016, 6pm

Where: Cliftons Brisbane, 288 Edward St, Brisbane City 4000.  Click here for a map.

Cost: Free - RSVP required. Click here to register

Presentation: 

In place of our normal Thursday evening meeting, the ATA Brisbane branch is attending this event conducted by the Environmental Defenders Office Queensland (website here).

"Are you hanging out for a sensible and responsible energy policy that protects our wildlife, agriculture and beautiful Queensland lifestyle?"

Join us for an in-depth discussion on how our State can overcome the hurdles currently blocking the renewable energy industry from taking off. This event will also help inform submissions on the Queensland climate change discussion paper, now due 2 September 2016.

Guest speakers include:

Trevor Berrill, Sustainable Energy Systems Consultant and Educator; and
John Grimes, Chief Executive of the Australian Solar Council.
