The Wright Home – Architecture and Engineering at its Best

The September usual Thursday meeting at the Albion Peace Centre is being replaced by a Saturday field trIp to Ian Wright’s new “Eco Casa” home at Wynnum. This is a field trip not to be missed. It’s a great example of best practice engineering and architecture. The home combines innovative eco composite construction of the building shell with energy and water efficiency, and of course, renewable energy power with battery storage.

Composite construction of homes is not common in Australia. But with Ian’s long experience in the boat building industry, as director of world renown Norman R Wright and Son’s, where using composite materials is common,  it was a logical application.

So come along and hear Ian explain the many features of this beautifully crafted, innovative home.

Kind regards, ATA Brisbane Branch.
