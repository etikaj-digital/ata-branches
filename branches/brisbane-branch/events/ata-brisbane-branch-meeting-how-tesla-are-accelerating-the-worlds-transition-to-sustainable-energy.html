ATA Brisbane branch meeting - How Tesla are accelerating the world’s transition to sustainable energy

  • 2017-10-26 08:30:00
  • 102 McDonald Rd, Windsor, QLD

When: Thursday 26 October 2017, 7:15pm for 7:30pm start

Where: Albion Peace Centre, 102 McDonald Rd, Windsor (underneath the Albion Overpass, near Blackmore St; UBD 140 Ref. C14)  Click here for a map.

Cost: $2, to help cover venue hire costs.

Presentation:  Tesla is big in the news and for good reason. Their innovative technology and approach have gained them a lot of attention. We will have a Tesla representative presenting. Starting with the founding of Tesla in 2003 and expanding on each of Tesla’s products and their purpose from the Tesla Roadster in 2003 to the world’s largest Lithium-ion battery revealed earlier this year.

For further details contact Trevor Berrill 0400 177 283 or  brisbane@ata.org.au
