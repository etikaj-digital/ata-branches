Smart Grid Interactive PV systems using storage and micro-inverters

John Harding has been working in the electronics industry for many years and since 2000 on the design and development of smart grid interactive PV systems. This includes micro-inverters and storage. His work has been in conjunction with ENERGEX, ELEVAR and his own company, MicroSolar Pty Ltd.

John will outline the research and product/systems development work his company is doing to bring about leading edge design in smart communications/control of PV systems, to maximise the benefits PV with storage and micro-inverters. This is a rapidly developing sector with companies such as Enphase already offering modular micro-inverter PV systems with battery storage.
