ATA Brisbane branch meeting - Renewables history in QLD &amp; Developments in energy rating labeling

  • 2016-02-25 08:30:00
  • 102 McDonald Rd, Windsor

When: Thursday 25 February 2016, 7:15pm for 7:30pm start

Where: Albion Peace Centre, 102 McDonald Rd, Windsor (underneath the Albion Overpass, near Blackmore St; UBD 140 Ref. C14)  Click here for a map.

Cost: $2, to help cover venue hire costs.

This month’s meeting will include talks by Trevor Berrill and Eric Peterson

Renewables in the Sunshine State – A Short Illustrated History (since 1970s) by Trevor Berrill

Trevor Berrill is a sustainable energy systems consultant with 40 years’ experience in renewables. His work has covered many aspects of renewables and energy efficiency including system design and installation (PV, wind, solar hot water and energy efficient buildings), training and education, research at UQ (wind energy and solar thermal), GU (engineering education) and QUT(solar thermal, energy efficient buildings and appliance efficiency).

Developments in labelling for air-conditioners, solar hot water system rating and house energy rating. Buyer and renters beware!  by Eric Peterson

Eric Peterson has a Ph.D in mechanical engineering and now works in private consultancy. Until recently he was an adjunct senior fellow and researcher at the Department of Civil Engineering, University of Queensland. His research work included solar desalination and energy efficient buildings.
