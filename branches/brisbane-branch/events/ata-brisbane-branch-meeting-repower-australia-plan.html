ATA Brisbane Branch meeting - Repower Australia Plan

  • 2018-08-23 09:30:00
  • 102 McDonald Rd, Windsor, QLD

When: Thursday 23 August 2018, 7:15pm for 7:30pm start

Where: Albion Peace Centre, 102 McDonald Rd, Windsor (underneath the Albion Overpass, near Blackmore St; UBD 140 Ref. C14)  Click here for a map.

Cost: $2, to help cover venue hire costs.

Presentation:

Jan McNicol is a climate activist with a strong interest in the constructive alternatives to the fossil fuel economy, including renewable energy. She has undertaken a project of talking to groups and decision makers about the Homegrown Power Plan initially and now the plan to Repower Australia with 100% renewable energy by 2030. Repower Australia is led by a consortium of groups such as Climate Action Network Australia, ACF, Nature Conservation Council, Environment Victoria, GetUp, AYCC, Solar Citizens and 350.org.

We hope to see you there.
