Brisbane branch

  • Overview
  • Events

Meetings of the Brisbane branch are on the fourth Thursday of each month at 7:30pm. There are no meetings in December and January.

If you are keen to be involved in supporting the local Renew branch and spreading the word about sustainability and renewable energy then we’d love to hear from you.

If you aren’t an Renew member then you can sign up to get email event notices from the Renew Brisbane branch using the form here. Renew members in Queensland are automatically notified of the Brisbane branch event through our member email bulletins.

If you’d like to investigate becoming a member of Renew click here.

You can read Trevor Berrill’s submission to the QLD Renewable Energy Expert Panel here.

Contact the branch via email: brisbane@ata.org.au
