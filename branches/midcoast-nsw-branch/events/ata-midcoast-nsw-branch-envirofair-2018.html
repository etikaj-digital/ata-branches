ATA Midcoast NSW branch - Envirofair 2018

  • 2018-06-08 23:00:00
  • Corner of Florence St and Wynter St, Taree, NSW

When: Saturday 9 June 2018, 9:00 am - 3:30 pm
Where: Taree Park, Corner of Florence St and Wynter St, Taree, NSW. Click here for a Google map.
Cost: Gold coin donation to Envirofair.

Come and meet the new Midcoast NSW branch of the ATA. We\'re excited to be attending Envirofair as our first \'official\' activity.

Envirofair hosted by our friends at 2BOB Radio (104.7FM). It\'s a lively community event that is in it\'s 27th year (!) and is focused on environmental awareness and how we can be a part of positive solutions.

  • entertainment
  • market stalls (including our ATA stall)
  • displays & demonstrations (environmental issues, solar for your home etc)
  • local artisans, craft, music
  • fresh organic produce and other food vendors
  • kids activities & workshops (candle & kite making, circus skills, and face painting, \'Junk Music\'

 

We hope to see you there,

Greg Berry and the ATA Midcoast NSW branch committee.
