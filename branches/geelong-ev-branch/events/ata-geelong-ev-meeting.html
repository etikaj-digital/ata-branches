ATA Geelong EV meeting

  • 2018-04-06 09:00:00
  • 33 Mt. Pleasant Rd, Belmont VIC 3216

When: Friday 6 April 2018, 7pm

Where: South Barwon Community Centre, 33 Mt. Pleasant Rd, Belmont VIC 3216. Click here for a map.

Cost: A gold coin donation to help with costs.

We will have an update on recent activity, upcoming EV events and updates from members regarding their EV conversions.

If you have come across interesting EV-related articles or news items during the month then forward details to geelongev@ata.org.au so that they can be shared at the meeting. You can also send along photos relating to your EV project progress.

We hope to see you at the meeting.

Blake Ramsey

Convenor, ATA Geelong EV Branch
