ATA Geelong EV branch - film night \&quot;The Future of Energy\&quot;

  • 2014-08-08 09:00:00
  • 33 Mt. Pleasant Rd, Belmont VIC 3216

Where: South Barwon Community Centre, 33 Mt. Pleasant Rd, Belmont VIC 3216. Click here for a map.

Cost: A voluntary donation to support the work of the film makers.

This is an invitation to a night at the movies.

We will be showing ‘The Future of Energy", a film on the renewable energy revolution -

Made by two young guys who crowdfunded $25k to fund the entire documentary.

Doug and Mick Aidt (involved with Transition East Geelong group and host of "The Sustainable Hour" on The Pulse) have secured the right to screen it for interested people.

Sorry about the short notice, be the venue availability became a factor.  If there is enough interest, we can have another screening some time in the future.

Please bring anyone with you who may be interested in this topic – treat it as an educational event.

To ensure we have enough seating available, please get back to me before Friday with details of how many people will be included in your group.

Hope to see you at the movies,

Peter

ATA Geelong EV Branch Committee and Peter Reeves Chairman,
