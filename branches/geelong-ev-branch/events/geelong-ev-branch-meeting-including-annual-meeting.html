Geelong EV branch meeting (including Annual Meeting)

  • 2014-09-05 09:00:00
  • 33 Mt. Pleasant Rd, Belmont VIC 3216

When: Friday 5 September 2014, 7pm

Where: South Barwon Community Centre, 33 Mt. Pleasant Rd, Belmont VIC 3216. Click here for a map.

Cost: A gold coin donation to help with costs.

This meeting will be followed by our Annual Meeting, where all positions on the committee will be declared vacant.
These positions will be filled, following nominations from the floor and elections, if necessary.
The current committee consists of Peter Reeves (Chairman), Andrew Van Slageren (Secretary), Kevin Leach (Treasurer), Doug Rolfe (Committee), Michael Axtens (Committee), Alastair Ryan (Committee).

At this stage, there may be an EV workshop on the following Saturday (September 6).
Confirmation of this workshop will be made at the meeting on Friday and notification will be included with the minutes, later that night.

Hope to see you at the meeting,
Peter
