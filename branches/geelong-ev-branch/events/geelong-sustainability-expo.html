Geelong Sustainability Expo

  • 2014-02-08 10:00:00
  • 50 Little Malop St, Geelong VIC 3220

Discover solutions for a sustainable, abundant community.

Come, see and experience stalls and demonstrations from a range of sustainability groups, and businesses.  Including; local, organic food, community gardens, renewable energy, electric vehicles, and heaps more.

Where: Geelong Performing Arts Centre - Foyer, 50 Little Malop St, Geelong VIC 3220

Cost: Free

Please come, learn and support sustainability initiatives in Geelong.

SOME GREAT REASONS TO COME TO THIS EVENT!!

  • FREE "Sustainable Geelong Directory" for the first 50 attendee\'s.
  • 21 Local groups and businesses represent - please come and support them.
  • Locally converted Electric Cars on display from the ATA Geelong Electric Vehicle branch
  • Some great music will be playing, slideshows and other info too.
  • The GPAC Cafe will be open all morning for some great food / coffee\'s
  • Some comfy chairs and lounges to catch up with friends / have a chat
  • The \'Transition Library\' will be on display and available to members; there\'s some great titles.
  • Great \'Incredible Edibles\' display in the courtyard.
