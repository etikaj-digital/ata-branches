Geelong EV Branch - January social meeting

  • 2014-01-03 18:00:00

Please be advised that our next meeting, on Friday January 3rd, will be a more informal event, and will be held at our workshop.

It will take the form of a BBQ dinner at around 6:00pm, followed by a casual meeting at around 8:00pm.

We will provide the meat, but it would be appreciated if everyone could bring a salad to serve with the BBQ’d meat, or a desert that can be shared amongst everyone who chooses to attend. You will also need to bring your own chair.

To ensure the correct amount of meat, bread and drinks are provided, I must ask that you confirm your attendance, via email, by next Thursday.

Feel free to bring anyone with you, who may be interested in joining us at this social gathering – just let me know.

The minutes from the meeting can be found here.
