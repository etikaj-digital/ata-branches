Geelong EV Branch

  • Overview
  • Events

Purpose

This branch is to provide a forum for anyone interested in Electric Vehicles, including bicycles, cars and other forms of electric transport.

Format

There is a monthly (60-90 minute) meeting where matters pertaining to electric vehicles are discussed. The formal part of the night is followed by an informal gathering for tea, coffee and biscuits and a chance to catch up with like minded people. There is no need to book and everyone (Renew member or not) is most welcome to attend.

Meeting details

Meetings are held on the 1st Friday of each month: 7:00pm at the South Barwon Community Centre, 33 Mt. Pleasant Rd, Belmont VIC. A gold coin donation is collected to cover refreshments. If travelling by public transport the venue is on bus route #42.
