Sydney West branch - Earthcare Fair

  • 2014-10-11 00:00:00
  • Cnr Science Rd and Campus Drive, Richmond NSW

When: Saturday 11 October, 10:00am - 4:00pm

Where: Earthcare Centre, UWS, Cnr Science Rd and Campus Drive, Richmond NSW

EarthCare Spring Flyer 2014 copy

  • Bike powered fruit smoothies
  • solar powered cookers
  • earth building displays.
  • RawLao food will demonstrate tasty vegetarian cooking
  • UWS Greenthumbs creating
  • organic pizzas in the wood fired cob oven
  • german cuisine served in traditional costume!
  • Natural beauty products; aromatherapy oils & massage
  • Sahaja yoga meditation
  • beautiful gem stones from a local collector
  • See local spinners in action with beautiful knits for sale
  • The Piggery Art Studio hosts their Spring Art Show with pottery, paintings and jewellery.
  • Live music organised by Lucia with the Chorella Choir starting at 10.30am; Green Tea Serenade;
  • Hawkesbury\'s own Ukulele band and Angus Findlay & friends.
  • Kids\' fun with arts & craft, puppet making, face painting & adventures through the garden!
