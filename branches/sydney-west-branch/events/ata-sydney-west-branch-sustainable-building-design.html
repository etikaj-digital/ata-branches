ATA Sydney West branch - Sustainable Building Design

  • 2018-06-02 03:30:00
  • Corner Campus Drive and Science Rd, Richmond, NSW

When: Saturday 2 June 2018, 1.30pm-2.30pm
Where: Hawkesbury Earthcare Centre, UWS, Corner Campus Drive and Science Rd, Richmond, NSW. Click here for a map.
Cost: $20.00. Afternoon tea provided
Registrations: Ph 6238 1665 or info@earthcare.org.au

Presenter:

Ray Trappel, an architect with extensive knowledge, experience and expertise in building design will provide advice on how to design a comfortable building with low impact on the environment. Participants will find out about:

  • passive design for a more energy efficient building
  • layout, building materials, thermal mass and construction type
  • design principles for climate zones
  • glazing, ventilation and insulation

We hope to see you there.

Jenny Dibley, Convenor
