Sydney West branch - Sustainability Lecture Series - Earth Building

  • 2015-08-01 03:30:00
  • Cnr Science Rd and Campus Drive, Richmond NSW

When: Saturday 1 Aug, 1:30pm - 3:30pm

Where: Earthcare Centre, UWS, Cnr Campus Drive and Beef Cattle Rd, Richmond, NSW. Click here for a map.

Cost: $10.00

Bookings: Call: 02 6238 1665 or email: info@earthcare.org.au

Speaker: Ray Trappel is an architect with extensive knowledge, experience and expertise in earth building. The lecture will cover all aspects of earth building ranging from methods and techniques to thermal comfort and embodied energy. The presentation will include a tour of the EarthCare building and the newly constructed \'Secret Garden\' building in the grounds of University of Western Sydney, Richmond.
