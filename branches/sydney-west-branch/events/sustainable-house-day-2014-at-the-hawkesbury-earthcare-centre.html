Sustainable House Day 2014 at The Hawkesbury EarthCare Centre

  • 2014-09-07 00:00:00
  • Cnr Science Rd and Campus Drive, Richmond NSW

When: Sunday 7 September 2014 , 10.00 am – 4.00 pm

The Hawkesbury EarthCare Centre at University of Western Sydney, Richmond, will be open to the public on Sustainable House Day.

The centre features a solar passive earth community building and extensive permaculture gardens.

Visitors can tour the site and see:

  • Walls constructed from rammed earth, mudbrick and strawbale
  • A straw panel ceiling and a composting toilet.
  • Interiors constructed  from recycled timber
  • An extensive organic garden covering over half a hectare (including composting)
  • A small dam, windmill and storage tank as part of the on-site water management system
  • A bread/pizza oven made from mud bricks
  • A variety of displays

There will be information stalls and items for sale (books, plants, seeds, solar powered devices, fruit and vegetables) and people will be available to answer questions.

The Hawkesbury Earthcare Centre is managed by the Alternative Technology Association (ATA) www.ata.org.au and Henry Doubleday Research Association (HDRA).
