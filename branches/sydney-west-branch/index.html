The branch meets on the first Saturday of each month from 10am at the Hawkesbury EarthCare Centre, University of Western Sydney, Richmond, all welcome.

The Centre features extensive permaculture and native gardens, water tanks and a solar passive community building constructed from strawbale, rammed earth and mud bricks. The Hawkesbury Artist Group and the Henry Doubleday Research Association are also located at the centre. The Centre is located on the corner of Science Rd and Campus Dr. On weekends please enter the campus via Londonderry Rd.
