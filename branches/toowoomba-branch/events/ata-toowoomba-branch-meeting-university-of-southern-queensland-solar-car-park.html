ATA Toowoomba branch meeting - University of Southern Queensland Solar Car Park

  • 2017-10-11 07:00:00
  • Corner Victoria and Herries Streets, Toowoomba, QLD

When: Wednesday 11 October 2017, 5:30pm for 6:00pm start.
Where: Toowoomba City Library, Level 3, Corner Victoria and Herries Streets, Toowoomba. Click here for a Google map.

Cost: Entry by gold coin donation

RSVP: Limited Seats. RSVP by 9 October 2017 to toowoomba@ata.org.au or 0419 736 219

Presenters: Alicia Logan, Manager, Environmental Manager, USQ Environmental Office and Andreas Helwig, Lecturer in Electro-Mechanical Engineering, USQ.

Hear about the largest solar carpark in Australia. Not only is shade and protection provided for thousands of vehicles but over a megawatt of power is generated free from the sun. Why don’t we have more of these dotted about Toowoomba and the nation at shopping centres and hospitals?

We look forward to seeing you there,

Mark Tranter

ATA Toowoomba Branch Convenor, 0419 736 219, toowoomba@ata.org.au
