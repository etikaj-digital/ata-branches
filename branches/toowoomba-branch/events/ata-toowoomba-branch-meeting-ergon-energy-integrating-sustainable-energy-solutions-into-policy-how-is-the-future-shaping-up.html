ATA Toowoomba branch meeting - Ergon Energy: Integrating sustainable energy solutions into policy. How is the future shaping up?

  • 2017-07-12 07:30:00
  • Corner Victoria and Herries Streets, Toowoomba, WLD

When: Wednesday 12 July 2017, 5pm for 5:30pm start.
Where: Toowoomba City Library, Level 3, Corner Victoria and Herries Streets, Toowoomba. Click here for a Google map.

Cost: Entry by gold coin donation

RSVP: By 10 July 2017 to toowoomba@ata.org.au or 0419 736 219

Presenter: Greg Caldwell, RPEQ, CPEng, Manager Network Strategy & Policy

New and emerging energy technologies such as solar PV, home battery storage, home energy management systems and electric vehicles will shape our future. Ergon Energy is leading the way with its latest connection standards to integrate these technologies into the network and leverage emerging solutions to manage network constraints.

We look forward to seeing you there,

Mark Tranter

ATA Toowoomba Branch Convenor, 0419 736 219, toowoomba@ata.org.au
