Renew Toowoomba Branch meeting - Ethical Investment

  • 2018-10-10 08:00:00
  • Corner Victoria and Herries Streets, Toowoomba, QLD

When: Wednesday 10 October 2017, 5.30pm for 6:00pm start.
Where: Toowoomba City Library, Level 3, Corner Victoria and Herries Streets, Toowoomba. Click here for a Google map.
Cost: Entry by gold coin donation
RSVP: By 8 October 2018 to toowoomba@ata.org.au or 0419 736 219

Note: Our annual meeting will be held on Wednesday 14 November, 7pm in the Toowoomba Library.

Presentation:

Do you know where your superannuation funds are being invested?

Would you like to see your investment going to make a cleaner, greener, more equal world?

The Toowoomba Branch of Renew (formerly Alternative Technology Association) is hosting a public seminar to help Toowoomba residents understand what ethical investing is and how they may be more proactive about ensuring the types of activities which their investments fund align with their values.

Speaker: Louise Edkins of Ethical Investment Advisers has more than twenty years experience in giving ethical investment advice. She is a certified financial planner and accredited Responsible investment association advisor.

We look forward to seeing you there,

Mark Tranter

ATA Toowoomba Branch Convenor, 0419 736 219, toowoomba@ata.org.au
