ATA Toowoomba branch - Turning off the electricity grid to your home - Hybrid PV systems

  • 2015-03-24 07:30:00
  • Toowoomba City Library, 618 Ruthven St, Toowoomba

When: Tuesday 24 March 2015, 6:30pm (doors open 6:15pm)

Where: Toowoomba City Library, 618 Ruthven St, Toowoomba

Cost: Tickets are free and available from the help desk at the City Library (no phone bookings). A light supper will be provided.

Presenter: Stan Jonsson from the  Solar Centre is a Clean Energy Council accredited installer/designer for off-grid solar PV systems and an accredited designer of grid connected residential and commercial solar PV systems.
