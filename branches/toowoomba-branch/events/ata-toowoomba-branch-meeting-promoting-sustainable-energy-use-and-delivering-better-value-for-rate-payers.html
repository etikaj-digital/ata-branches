ATA Toowoomba branch meeting - Promoting sustainable energy use and delivering better value for rate payers

  • 2018-05-09 08:00:00
  • Corner Victoria and Herries Streets, Toowoomba, QLD

When: Wednesday 9 May 2017, 5.30pm for 6:00pm start.
Where: Toowoomba City Library, Level 3, Corner Victoria and Herries Streets, Toowoomba. Click here for a Google map.

Cost: Entry by gold coin donation

RSVP: By 5 May 2018 to toowoomba@ata.org.au or 0419 736 219

Presenters: Jaek Passier, Principal Engineer, Toowoomba Regional Council, and Dr Sonia Marshall, Strategic Policy Officer, Sunshine Coast Regional Council (by video link).

Hear about the current sustainable energy initiatives being implemented by the Toowoomba and Sunshine Coast Regional Councils.

How will the new Virtual Power Plant manage TRC’s peak demand and reduce power costs?

Will the Sunshine Coast’s Solar Farm really power 5,000 homes & meet ALL the Council’s power needs?

We look forward to seeing you there,

Mark Tranter

ATA Toowoomba Branch Convenor, 0419 736 219, toowoomba@ata.org.au
