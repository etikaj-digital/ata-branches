ATA Toowoomba branch meeting- New Energy for the Future - Household Battery Technology

  • 2016-04-12 08:00:00
  • Corner Victoria and Herries Streets, Toowoomba

When: Tuesday 12 April 2015, 6:00pm

Where: Toowoomba City Library, Level 3, Corner Victoria and Herries Streets, Toowoomba

Cost: Gold coin donation. Tea, coffee and biscuits supplied.

RSVP: Limited seats. Please RSVP to toowoomba@ata.org.au or phone 0419 736 219

Presentation:

Are you interested in becoming more self-sufficient as far as electricity is concerned? There has been much talk about cheap household batteries like the Tesla Powerwall.

Come and hear what the present situation is with this technology.

Presented by Andreas Helwig Lecturer in electro-mechanical engineering at USQ and Stan Jonsson installer of battery/PV systems including the Tesla Powerwall.

You can download Andreas\' presentation here.

We look forward to seeing you there,
