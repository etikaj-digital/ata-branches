ATA Toowoomba - Start-up Meeting

  • 2015-01-17 23:00:00
  • 5 Goodwood St, Toowoomba.

When: Sunday 18 January 2015, 10:00am

Where: 5 Goodwood St, Toowoomba.

RSVP: doug@ata.org.au

This meeting is for ATA members interested in hearing plans for the new Toowoomba branch of the Alternative Technology Association (ATA).

ATA members form a vibrant network of people actively reducing their impact on the environment by making tangible changes to their homes and lifestyles, and sharing skills with people in their area. ATA branches are involved in activities like sustainable house tours, organising informative seminars and workshops and attending fairs and events. Electric vehicles, solar panels, energy efficiency, green home design and rainwater tanks are just some of the topics ATA branches explore.

Meeting plan:

  • Opening, background and welcome
  • Guest Speaker: Sustainable Housing
  • Plans for first public meeting
  • Chat and coffee

 

We look forward to seeing you there,

Interim Committee: Mark Tranter, Ross Campbell, Don Carlson
