ATA Toowoomba branch - Annual Meeting

  • 2018-02-21 07:00:00
  • 80 Russell St, Toowoomba City QLD 4350

When: Wednesday 21 February 2018, 6.00pm
Where: Tara Room, Irish Club Hotel, 80 Russell St, Toowoomba City QLD 4350. Click here for a Google map.
RSVP 12/02/2018: toowoomba@ata.org.au or ph. 0419 736 219

2017 was another successful year for the Toowoomba ATA Branch. A couple of highlights being a field trip to the Gatton UQ solar farm and a developing interest in community owned renewable energy.

You are cordially invited to attend the Toowoomba Branch Annual Meeting.

Business includes:
- Executive reports
- Election of office bearers
- Planning for 2018

Following the meeting you are invited to join other members for dinner and further discussions. There will also be a report about the presentation to Council on the Cities Power Partnership happening on 7 February.

Mark Tranter

ATA Toowoomba Branch Convenor, 0419 736 219, toowoomba@ata.org.au
