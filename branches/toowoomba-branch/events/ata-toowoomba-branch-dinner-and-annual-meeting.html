ATA Toowoomba branch - Dinner and Annual Meeting

  • 2017-01-31 07:00:00
  • 80 Russell St, Toowoomba QLD 4350

When: Tuesday 31 January 2017, 6pm
Where: Irish Club Hotel (Tara Room), 80 Russell St, Toowoomba QLD 4350
RSVP: By 30/01/2017,  toowoomba@ata.org.au or ring 0419 736 219

2016 was a very successful year for the Toowoomba ATA Branch: well attended public meetings, a very successful sustainable house day, and the very popular electric vehicle expo.

You are cordially invited to attend the Toowoomba Branch Annual General meeting.

Business includes:
- Executive reports
- Election of office bearers
- Planning for 2017

Following the meeting you are invited to join other members for dinner and further discussions.

You can read the Convenor’s Annual Report for 2016 here.

Mark Tranter

ATA Toowoomba Branch Convenor, 0419 736 219, toowoomba@ata.org.au
