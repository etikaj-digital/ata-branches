World Renewable Energy Congress - public meeting - Can we have 100% renewable energy?

  • 2017-02-05 06:00:00
  • 179 High Street, Fremantle, WA

When: Sunday 5 February 2017, 5pm
Where: Victoria Hall, 179 High Street, Fremantle. Click here for a map.
Cost: Free

To kick off the year, the ATA Perth branch is inviting all local ATA members to attend this important public meeting on the topic of running our society on 100% renewable energy.

The World Renewable Energy Congress XVI is being hosted at Murdoch University and as a part of their social program they have included this public meeting. Details of the meeting can be found here.

"The conference will host a public meeting with speakers from various regions. As a public event, this will give delegates the opportunity to interact with the locals with similar interests in the field.

A local personality will MC the meeting which will consist of short presentations from a panel of four to be opened up to the floor for questions, comments and discussion."

This is a great opportunity to become more informed about this crucial topic and to meet some fellow ATA members prior to the regular branch activities starting again in March.

We hope to see you at the meeting,
ATA Perth branch Convenor,
Travis Hargreaves, perthbranch@ata.org.au
