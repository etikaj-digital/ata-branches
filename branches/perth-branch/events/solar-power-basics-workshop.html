Solar Power Basics Workshop

  • 2016-02-13 02:00:00
  • 11 Wentworth Pde, Success, WA

When: Saturday, 13 February 2016,  1:00 pm- 3:30 pm

Where: Success Library, 11 Wentworth Pde, Success

Considering installing solar photovoltaics? Then join Travis Hargreaves from the Alternative Technology Association who will share his expertise on all things solar.

Learn about:

  • What to look for in your energy bills
  • What to look for when purchasing a solar power system and choosing an installer
  • How to size a system for your house
  • What questions to ask your installer before you commit
