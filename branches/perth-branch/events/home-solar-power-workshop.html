Home Solar Power Workshop

  • 2016-03-03 07:30:00
  • 22 Bolero Road, Aveley 6069
When: Thursday 3 March 2016, 6.30pm - 8.30pm
Where: Aveley Community Centre, 22 Bolero Road, Aveley 6069. Click here for a map.
RSVP: Nina at environment@emrc.org.au or 9424 2216 ** Places are limited
Considering installing solar photovoltaics? Then join Travis Hargreaves from the Alternative Technology Association who will share his expertise on all things solar.

Interested in solar power for your home but not sure where to start? Travis Hargreaves from the Alternative Technology Association will walk you through the essentials of what you need to know and help you begin your journey to a new, ‘greener’ way of living.

Hosted by the Eastern Metropolitan Regional Council.
