Australia: Renewable Energy Superpower?

  • 2016-10-27 06:45:00
  • Cnr Stirling Hwy and Hackett Drive, Crawley, WA 6009
When: Thursday 27 October from 5:45-8:00pm
Where: Social Sciences Lecture Theatre Room G130, University of Western Australia, Cnr Stirling Hwy and Hackett Drive, Crawley, WA 6009. Click here for a map.
Cost: Free
ATA members in the Perth area are invited to join members of the local branch in attending the launch of Beyond Zero Emissions\' (BZE) "Renewable Energy Superpower" Report. See full details and register to attend this free event here.
There will be an introductory keynote presentation by The Hon Mark Butler MP, ALP Shadow Minister for Climate Change and Energy and National President of the Australian Labor Party along with a Q&A panel.
"Renewable Energy Superpower is the fifth part in the acclaimed Zero Carbon Australia series, detailing full and rapid decarbonisation of Australia\'s stationary energy, buildings, transport, land use, export and industrial sectors. Zero Carbon Australia is a joint project between Beyond Zero Emissions  and The University of Melbourne\'s Energy Research Institute."
Other Events:
This Sunday 23 October is the Spring Eco Fest run by the Conservation Council of West Australia. It runs from 11am - 4pm. Full details here.
We hope to see you at the meeting,
