ATA Perth branch meeting - Electric Vehicles in Perth. An update on technology progress and vehicle battery afterlife.

  • 2018-07-02 09:00:00
  • 55 Salvado Road, Subiaco WA 6008

When: Monday 2 July , 2018, 7:00 - 9:00pm.
Where: Function Room, Home Base, 55 Salvado Road, Subiaco WA 6008. Click here for a Google map of the venue.
Cost: General public $6.50, free to ATA and AEVA members (gold coin donation welcome).
RSVP: Book your ticket now as there are limited seats for this event. Click here to book your ticket.

Presentations: Electric Vehicles in Perth. An update on technology progress and vehicle battery afterlife.

Leo Kerr, founder of Myelectriccar.com.au will bring you up to speed with the recent advancements and sweeping progress of the EV industry, specific to Perth.

Then KT Lim will give us an insight into the much talked about afterlife of EV batteries, sharing with us the progress update of his own “Battery Second life” project.

We will also be lucky enough to be joined by Donna Luckman (CEO of ATA). Donna will bring an update on ATA’s latest exciting ventures.

ATA Perth Branch invites members and guests to join us. We hope to see you on the evening.

ATA Perth Branch committee.
