Workshop - Solar Panel Basics

  • 2014-10-30 07:00:00
  • 61 Broun Avenue, Morley, WA

Where: Civic Centre, City of Bayswater, 61 Broun Avenue, Morley

When: Thursday 30 October 2014, 6pm-8.30pm

Cost: Free, snacks supplied.

Travis Hargreaves from the Perth branch of the ATA will be giving an introduction to solar PV systems as part of the City of Bayswater\'s series of community workshops. You can read more about the program here.

Learn the basics of solar PV. This workshop will be particularly useful for homeowners considering installing solar PV. You will find out about:

  • What to look for in your energy bills;
  • What to look for in a PV system and installer and how to size a system for your home; &
  • The questions to ask before you commit.
