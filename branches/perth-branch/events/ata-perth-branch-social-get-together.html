ATA Perth branch - Social get together

  • 2016-03-22 07:00:00
  • 51 Cantonment Street, Fremantle, WA

When: Tuesday 22 March, 6pm onwards

Where: Clancy\'s Fish Pub, 51 Cantonment Street, Fremantle – 5 minute walk from Fremantle train station

The Perth Branch of the ATA is having a social gathering in Fremantle later this month and we would love to see you there.

Meet with fellow members, bring friends, discuss sustainable projects and share stories.

This will be an informal gathering, so just turn up, and say hello - the more ATA members the better.

Upcoming Event: Electrikhana is back for 2016 -  The Australian Electric Vehicle Association (WA) is holding their electric vehicle expo and test-drive day at RAC DTEC (Driving Centre) - 16 Grogan Road, Perth International Airport. Gates open at 9 am. AEVA members and kids under 16 get in for free.  General entry is $5, test-drives/rides $10 (drivers and passengers will need to print and sign an indemnity form before circulating on the track). Tickets are available here.

Come along and experience the latest electric cars, bikes, scooters, e-bikes, skateboards, Segways, boats... you name it!  If it\'s electric, we\'ve got it!  A sausage sizzle will be running from 10 am with cold drinks and coffee available throughout the day.
