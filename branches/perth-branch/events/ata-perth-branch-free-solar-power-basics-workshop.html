ATA Perth Branch - Free Solar power basics workshop

  • 2015-09-17 08:30:00
  • 61 Broun Avenue, Morley

When: Thursday 17 September, 6.30-8pm.
Where: Civic Centre, 61 Broun Avenue, Morley
Costs: Free. refreshments provided.
RSVP ESSENTIAL: Call Nina on 9424 2216 or email environment@emrc.org.au

ATA\'s Branch Convenor, Travis Hargreaves will share expert tips on solar power at this workshop held in colloboration with the City of Bayswater. The workshop will be particularly useful for homeowners considering installing solar PV.

Find out:

  • what to look for in your energy bills
  • what to look for in a solar power system and installer and how to size a system for your home
  • the questions to ask before you commit.
