ATA Canberra branch - AIA Solar House Day Public Lecture \&quot;The future of housing in Canberra: What does it look like?\&quot;

  • 2018-07-25 08:00:00
  • 15 Gordon St, Acton ACT 2601
When: Wednesday 25 July 2018, Exhibition 4:00-6:00pm, Lecture 6:00-7:00 pm
Where: The Shine Dome, 15 Gordon St, Acton ACT 2601. Click here for a Google map.In keeping with recent tradition, the Canberra branch of the Alternative Technology Association is encouraging its members and friends to make tracks for the Solar House Day public lecture and exhibition presented by the Australian Institute of Architects (including a “speed dating" session with an an architect).

Note: For this reason there will be no regular July meeting of the Canberra branch of the ATA on that day.

This year the lecture will be given by Catherine Townsend (ACT Government Architect) and Antonio Mozqueira (Manager Climate Change at ACT Government).

The events are free, but RSVPs are required. Full details are available here. On the website you can reserve a seat for the lecture and also book seats for the bus tours (29th July and 5 August) of the ACT region\'s best climate responsive homes.

Should be a very informative event.
