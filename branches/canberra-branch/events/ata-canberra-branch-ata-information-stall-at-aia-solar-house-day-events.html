ATA Canberra branch - ATA information stall at AIA Solar House Day events

  • 2016-07-27 06:00:00
  • 15 Gordon Street, Acton, 2601
When: Wednesday 27 July, 2016, 4pm
Where: Shine Dome, 15 Gordon Street, Acton, 2601

Note: The ATA Canberra Branch will not be holding its normal presentation evening in July.

The ATA Canberra branch encourages all members and friends to attend the ATA information stall at the Solar House Day public lecture and expo, organised by the ACT chapter of the Australian Institute of Architects. Further details can be found at the AIA event webpage here.

Have a chat to ATA members who are also experienced architects and see a range of sustainable products and technologies. Then attend a presentation by four architects demonstrating different approaches to designing a solar house on difficult blocks of land.

The event is free but places are limited so please RSVP by clicking here.
