ATA Canberra meeting - Residential Gas Use - Is it financially and environmentally sound?

  • 2015-03-16 08:30:00
  • 31 North Road Acton ACT 2601

When: Monday 16 March 2015, 7:30pm

Where: Ian Ross Common Room and Seminar Room, Australian National University, 31 North Road
Acton ACT 2601

Speaker: Craig Memery is ATA’s energy consumer advocate and specialist in energy policy, regulation and markets. Craig’s experience over 12 years in energy includes economic and technical analysis, systems design, business development, project management, governance, community development, research, and training. With the support of the Consumer Advocacy Panel, Craig advocates on behalf of Australian energy consumers for affordable and sustainable supply and use of energy. Craig will present findings from ATA\'s new report Are We Still Cooking With Gas? Which examines whether gas has a future in Australian households.

This is a joint meeting with the ASC.

We look forward to seeing you there.

Trevor Lee,
ATA Canberra branch, Interim Convenor

Advance notice: The next meeting on Wednesday 29 April will be addressed by Shane Rattenbury MLA on the topic of "A Fair Price For Solar" and will include the General Meeting of ATA members for reinaugurating ATA Canberra Branch.
