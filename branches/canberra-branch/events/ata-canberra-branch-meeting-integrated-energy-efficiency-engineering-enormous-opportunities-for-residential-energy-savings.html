ATA Canberra branch meeting - Integrated Energy Efficiency Engineering - enormous opportunities for residential energy savings

  • 2015-09-30 09:30:00
  • 31 North Road Acton ACT 2601
When: Wednesday September 30th, 2015, 7:30 pm
Where: Ian Ross Building, Australian National University
Presentation:
Tim Edwards is the President of the Australian Refrigeration Association, a not for profit industry association in the Heating Ventilation Air Conditioning and Refrigeration (HVACR) industry that addresses all sectors and advocates for commercially and environmentally preferable technology. Global agreement for the phase down of hydrofluorocarbon (HFC) and the phase out of hydrochlorofluorocarbon (HCFC) refrigerants will enable dramatic improvements in HVACR energy efficiency and emissions reduction.
Integrated HVACR energy efficiency engineering has the potential to reduce the cost of HVACR services to Australia by $10B per annum and reduce national CO2e emissions by 7%.
The innovative technologies that contribute to these improvements and how they can be used to optimize energy efficiency in residential, commercial and industrial applications will be described.
 
This is a public meeting.  Please invite anyone who might be interested.
