ATA Canberra branch at AIA \&#039;Hot Housing\&#039; lecture, exhibition and tour.

  • 2017-07-26 06:00:00
  • 15 Gordon St, Acton, ACT

When: Wednesday, 26 July 2017
4:00– 6:00pm Exhibition
6:00 – 7:00 pm Lecture - Hot Housing
Where: "The Shine Dome", 15 Gordon St, Acton, ACT (Australian Academy of Science, ANU). Click here for a map.
Cost: Gold coin donation. Seating is limited so please RSVP at architecture.com.au/act

As our July activity, the ATA Canberra branch will be participating in the Australian Institute of Architects events around the theme of \'Hot Housing\'.

Exhibition (4:00– 6:00pm): Browse and talk with exhibitors of sustainable products and technologies. The ATA Canberra branch will be attending an running an ATA stall.

Lecture - Hot Housing (6:00 – 7:00 pm)
The year-round housing performance presentation will hear from architects and specialists about how a house should be designed so that it performs over the whole year, particularly when dealing with increased temperatures due to climate change.

Associated event: Solar House Day Bus Tour, Sunday 30 July 2017 - 10 am to 3 pm

A bus tour to view our region\'s best climate appropriate energy-efficient homes.

Meeting Point: ACT Chapter, 2a Mugga Way, Red Hill. A lunch break will be provided at the Fyshwick Fresh Food Markets

Cost: $55 Standard, $50 Concession. Please visit the AIA website architecture.com.au/act to buy tickets.

We hope to see you at this event,

Regards,

Andrew Perry and Peter Overton
