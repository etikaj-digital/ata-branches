ATA Canberra branch meeting - ACT Next Generation Energy Storage Program

  • 2016-06-29 09:30:00
  • 31 North Road Acton ACT 2601
When: Wednesday 29 June, 2016, 7:30 pm
Where: Ian Ross Building, Australian National University, 31 North Road, Acton ACT 2601
Speaker: Todd Eagles, Manager of Energy Efficiency and Renewable Energy Programs at ActewAGL
Subject: ActewAGL and the ACT Next Generation Energy Storage Program
Todd will present an overview of the key facts and aims of the current battery storage trial being run in the ACT and the role of ActewAGL in that program.There will then be ample time for Q&A on and around the topics of storage, energy use and metering.
