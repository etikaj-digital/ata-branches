ATA Canberra meeting - How Residential Solar and Storage will fundamentally change the power game

  • 2015-06-24 09:30:00
  • 31 North Road Acton ACT 2601

When: Wednesday 24 June 2015, 7:30pm.

Where: Ian Ross Common Room and Seminar Room, Australian National University, 31 North Road, Acton ACT 2601

Dean Spaccavento from Reposit Power is a technical expert in the electricity industry. Formerly the CIO of Energy Response, Dean intimately understands the electricity space and the high performance enterprise software required for modern grids. Dean has worked in North America and Asia Pacific. Reposit Power’s automated software links solar panels and storage, to make them work in harmony, and then sells back to the grid when prices are high.

As usual there will be time for relaxed discussion after the formal presentations.
