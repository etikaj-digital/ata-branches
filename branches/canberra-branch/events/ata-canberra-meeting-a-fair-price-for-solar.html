ATA Canberra meeting - A Fair Price For Solar

  • 2015-04-29 08:15:00
  • 31 North Road Acton ACT 2601

When: Wednesday 29 April 2015

Time : 7:15 to 7:45 pm: ATA General Meeting, 8:00pm Public meeting addressed by Shane Rattenbury MLA

Where: Ian Ross Common Room and Seminar Room, Australian National University, 31 North Road
Acton ACT 2601

ATA Canberra Branch - General Meeting: 7:15 to 7:45 pm

A short meeting for ATA members to re-inaugurate the ATA Canberra branch and for voting in the new committee.

Shane Rattenbury MLA, Subject: "A Fair Price For Solar" Time: 8:00 pm

Well know for his strong support of renewable energy, Shane will outline the need for guaranteed, fair price for solar PV generated electricity in the ACT in the face of Federal Government and vested interests campaigning against paying local solar owners fairly for the clean power they produce.

This is a joint meeting with the ASC.

We look forward to seeing you there.

Trevor Lee,
ATA Canberra branch, Interim Convenor
