ATA Canberra Branch - The renewables industry in the south-east &amp; Real-time weather data

  • 2015-05-27 09:30:00
  • 31 North Road Acton ACT 2601

When: Wednesday 27 May April 2015, 7:30pm.

Where: Ian Ross Common Room and Seminar Room, Australian National University, 31 North Road, Acton ACT 2601

1.  Craig Hanicek, the SERREE Cluster Manager  will present on "The Role and Prospects for SERREE"

SERREE – the South East Region of Renewable Energy Excellence is an initiative to develop the renewable energy sector of the south east NSW-ACT region as an Industry Cluster. As SERREE Cluster Manager, Craig has been instrumental in building the SERREE Network to include over 450 members who together represent the broad diversity of the region’s renewable energy stakeholders – the three tiers of government, business and industry, research institutions, education providers and community organisations.

SERREE regularly facilitates events for members to meet to initiate new contacts, discuss issues, share information, and form strategic partnerships that progress opportunities and develop solutions. The SERREE Industry Cluster is supported by the Australian Renewable Energy Agency (ARENA), the NSW and ACT Governments, Regional Development Australia ACT and Southern Inland, and the private sector.

It will be very interesting to find out how Craig\'s work is proceeding.
2. Trevor Lee from Exemplary Energy Partners (exemplary.com.au) will discuss "Real time weather data for Canberra and other capitals".
There are many important applications of the real-time weather, solar and climate data in modelling and validating built structures and renewable energy systems among others. Come along to find out how they are generated and the full range of their uses.
As usual there will be time for relaxed discussion after the formal presentations.

This is a joint meeting with the ASC.
