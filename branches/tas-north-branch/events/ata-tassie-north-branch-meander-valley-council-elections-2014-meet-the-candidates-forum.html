ATA Tassie North branch - Meander Valley Council Elections 2014 - Meet the candidates forum

  • 2014-10-09 08:00:00
  • West Barrack Street, Deloraine, Tasmania

Where: Deloraine Uniting Church hall, West Barrack Street, Deloraine, Tasmania

When: Thursday 9 October 2014 at 7 p.m.

This year\'s Meander Valley council elections are more important than usual as we will elect councillors for the next 4 years, not two years as we have done before. Posters around the district show that there will be a few new faces as well as those who have previously served on the Council in other years.

We invite you to meet the candidates and ask questions of them, either during the forum or afterwards over tea and coffee.

Helen Hutchinson

ATA Tasmania North branch
(For John Phelps, Chairman, Deloraine Uniting Church Council).
