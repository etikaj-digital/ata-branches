ATA Tas North branch meeting - Meander Valley Council\&#039;s Bioenergy Project

  • 2017-06-19 07:30:00
  • 26 Lyall St, Westbury TAS 7303

When: Monday 19 June 2017, 5.30pm start with soup and bread rolls.
Where: Meander Valley Council Chambers, 26 Lyall St, Westbury TAS 7303. Click here for a map.

Cost: Free. Soup and bread rolls from 5.30 p.m. to quell some of those hunger pangs. Tea and coffee also available.

Presenter: Craig Plaisted will be presenting us the story of the Meander Valley Council bio-energy project.

We will be having a general meeting to confirm the branch committee for the upcoming year.

At this meeting I will be stepping down as Convenor and Sue will be stepping down as treasurer. So please think about whether you would like to take on either of these positions. We have a great team and there\'s lots of support, but we need to spread the load to enable us to keep putting on great local events. If you would like more information about any of the committee positions please either email or call me on 0438233576 (you can also talk to Doug the ATA Branch Coordinator on 03 9631 5407 or doug@ata.org.au)

We hope to see you at the meeting.

Helen Hutchinson, ATA Tas North Convenor
