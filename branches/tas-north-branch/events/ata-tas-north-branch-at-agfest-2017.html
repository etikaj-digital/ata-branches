ATA Tas North branch at AGFEST 2017

  • 2017-05-03 22:00:00
  • 415 Oaks Road, Carrick, Tasmania 7291
When: Thursday 4 - Saturday 6 May 2017
Where: 415 Oaks Road, Carrick, Tasmania 7291The ATA Tassie North branch will again be running a 3-day ATA stall at AGFEST. We will be sharing our site with the Tasmanian branch of the Australian Electric Vehicle Association (AEVA). It will be a fascinating and educational site with electric cars, hands on solar activities for kids etc and a full ATA stall of magazines and books.

Last year there were 60,000 patrons and 700 exhibitors. So this is a huge undertaking and extra volunteers would be most welcome. If you\'re already planning to attend AGFEST then please contact Helen to organise helping out on the ATA stand for an hour or two.
