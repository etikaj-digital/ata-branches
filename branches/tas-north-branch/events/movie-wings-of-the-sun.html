Movie - Wings of the Sun

  • 2015-04-22 09:30:00
  • 2 Meander Valley Road, Deloraine

Time to see something really Amazing!! Exciting!! Exhilarating!!

Not since Charles Lindbergh set off in May 20 1927 has there been such interest in a flight.  Beginning its journey on March 9 2015 the Solar Impulse 2 is attempting to be the world\'s first completely solar powered aircraft to fly around the world.  Come along to the Little Theatre on April 22nd and learn about the development, construction and first flights of this wonderful project.

Details

What:   "Wings of the Sun": a film about solar powered flight and our possible future

When:  7.30 p.m. - 9.30 p.m. Wednesday 22nd April, 2015

Where:  at the Performing Arts Centre, Deloraine (the Little Theatre)

Cost:    free, although you might like to donate a little towards the cost of the film and the theatre and equipment hire. Tell your friends about it!  Suitable for all ages.

Enquiries: phone Helen 0438 233 576
