ATA Tas North branch meeting - \&#039;From 1999 to now. A Solar and battery journey\&#039; AND Annual Meeting

  • 2018-07-29 02:00:00
  • Weymouth, TAS 7252

When: Sunday 29 July 2018. Annual meeting 12.00-12.30pm, main event 1.30pm
Where: Weymouth TAS 7252 (between Georgetown and Bridport). Click here for a Google map. Weymouth is approx 60km N of Launceston off the road between Georgetown and Bridport.
Cost: Free
RSVP is required to tasnorth@ata.org.au to get directions and parking instructions.

Guest: Martin Dingemanse from Mode Electrical

Please come along to Weymouth to visit Sue Woinarki’s home to find out about their solar and battery journey. Starting in 1999 with the second domestic solar setup in Tasmania progressing through to more panels in 2009, a gel battery installation in 2015 and a Sanden heat pump and more panels as well as upgraded inverter in 2017. Martin Dingemanse from Mode Electrical who installed Sue’s latest panels and inverters will be on hand to answer questions.

We will be having our Annual Meeting from 12 noon to which we welcome all to come along to. In between the Annual Meeting and the afternoon session there will be a light lunch.

We hope to see you at the meeting.

Sean Manners,

Convenor, ATA Tas North branch

tasnorth@ata.org.au
