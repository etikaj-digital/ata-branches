ATA Tassie North branch - film night \&#039;Just Eat It\&#039; - in conjunction with SLWG Tamar NRM

  • 2015-12-10 07:30:00
  • West Tamar Council, Eden Street, Riverside TAS 7250

When: Thursday 10 December 2015, 6:30pm

Where: West Tamar Council, Eden Street, Riverside

Cost: Entry is $5 on the night per film.

The ATA Tassie North branch is assisting the Sustainable Living Working Group of Tamar NRM to show "Just Eat It"

This film shows how much food is wasted from paddock to our own tables and asks us to consider options for change.
"We collectively waste nearly 50% of global food. Jen and Grant dive into the chain of food waste from farm ... to the back of the fridge."
