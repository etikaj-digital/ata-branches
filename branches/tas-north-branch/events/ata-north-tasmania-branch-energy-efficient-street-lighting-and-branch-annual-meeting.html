ATA North Tasmania branch - Energy efficient street lighting and branch Annual Meeting

  • 2016-05-23 07:30:00
  • Westbury Council Chambers, 26 Lyall St, Westbury TAS 7303

When: Monday 23 May 2016, 5:30pm.

Where: Westbury Council Chambers, 26 Lyall St, Westbury TAS 7303. Click here for a map.

As well as hearing about the success of our activities
in 2015-16, and confirming the office bearers for 2016-17, we will
hear from Craig Plaisted about street lighting initiatives in the
Meander Valley and the cost savings for Meander Valley residents.  We
will also discuss activities for the coming year, so it is your
chance to have input into what, why, where and how we do this.  If
you can stay after the meeting there will be a light supper and time
to chat with other members and friends.

Warm regards
Helen
Convenor, ATA Northern Tasmania.
