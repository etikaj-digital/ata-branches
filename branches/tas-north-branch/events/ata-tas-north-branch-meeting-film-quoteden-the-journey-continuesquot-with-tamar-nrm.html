ATA Tas North branch meeting - Film: \&quot;Eden: The Journey Continues\&quot; with Tamar NRM

  • 2016-09-20 08:00:00
  • Eden St. Riverside, TAS

When: Tuesday 20 September 2016, 6pm.
Where: West Tamar Council Chambers, Eden St. Riverside. Click here for a map.
Cost: Free. Tea and coffee will be available, please bring a plate for supper.

For the next meeting of the ATA Tas North branch members are invited to join Tamar NRM’s Sustainable Living Working Group for a showing of “Eden: The Journey Continues”.

The Eden Project was a Millenium Project in Cornwall in the UK. It sets out to illustrate humanity’s dependence on plants – for food and beverages, for shelter and clothing, for fuel and medicine, for rubber and paper, for rainfall and last but not least for oxygen. Plants useful to humanity are grown in three biomes – tropical, warm temperate and cool temperate outdoors. The first two are in climate controlled geodesic domes. There is also an education centre that discusses everything you never thought of about energy. The film depicts the planning, building and stocking of the Project up to and including its tenth birthday celebrations.

We hope to see you there.
