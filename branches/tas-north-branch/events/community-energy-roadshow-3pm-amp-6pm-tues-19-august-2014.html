Community Energy Roadshow, 3pm &amp; 6pm, Tues 19 August 2014

  • 2014-08-19 05:00:00
  • 64 High Street Sheffield TAS 7306

Where: Old Gallery (behind Service Tas), High St, Sheffield, TAS

When: 3:00 pm & 6:00 pm, Tuesday 19 August 2014

Cost: Free

Representatives from community groups in Tasmania went to Canberra recently to take part in the first Australian Community Energy Congress.

This Roadshow is to share some of what they learned with you! Hear what other towns in Australia and around the world are doing to harness renewable energy to power their communities.

Questions: Shayn 0419905482 email: shaynharkness AT gmail.com

The Roadshow is a collaborative effort supported by Sustainable Living Tasmania, KEENER Inc., Alternative Technology Association (ATA) Tas North Branch, Hydro Tasmania and the Coalition for Community Energy (C4CE).
