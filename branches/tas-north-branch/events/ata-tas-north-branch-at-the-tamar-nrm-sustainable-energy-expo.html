ATA Tas North branch at the Tamar NRM Sustainable Energy Expo

  • 2017-09-15 23:00:00
  • 47 Tamar St &amp; Cimitere St, Launceston, TAS

When: Saturday 16 September 2017, 9.00am - 4.00pm
Where: Albert Hall, 47 Tamar St & Cimitere St, Launceston. Click here for a map.
Cost: Free

The ATA Tas North branch will be teaming up again with the local branch of the Australian EV Association to run a stall at the Tamar NRM Sustainable Energy Expo.

  • Showcasing sustainable energy options to reduce costs and benefit the environment
  • Presenting the benefits of sustainable building design and sustainable living
  • Showing the pathways when converting to a sustainable energy source
  • Demonstrating energy saving ideas in homes and business
  • Demonstrating technologies such as wind and solar
  • Promoting local business and local knowledge

Along with Sustainable House Day on Sunday 17th September, this will be a great weekend packed with information on sustainable living
