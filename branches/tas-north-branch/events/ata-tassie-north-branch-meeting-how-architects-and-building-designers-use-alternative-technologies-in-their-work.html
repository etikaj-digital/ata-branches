ATA Tassie North branch meeting - How architects and building designers use alternative technologies in their work

  • 2016-08-29 08:30:00
  • 85 Meander Valley Rd, Westbury, TAS

When: Monday 29 August 2016, 6.30pm

Where: 85 Meander Valley Rd, Westbury.  Next door to the Community Health Centre on the Launceston side, opposite the Westbury Cricket Oval. Look out for the balloons.

Subject: How architects and building designers use alternative technologies in their work.

Heritage architect Gayle Plunkett, based in Deloraine and young building designer Freya Su from Snug House, Launceston will be talking about alternate technologies that they use in their work and the challenges that can raise. Freya will be using a recent straw bale build in Launceston to illustrate some of Snug House’s work.

(There may also be a third architect from Hobart if it is able to be arranged).

Supper, tea and coffee provided. Please come along.
