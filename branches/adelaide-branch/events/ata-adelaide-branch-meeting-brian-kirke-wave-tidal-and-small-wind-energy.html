ATA Adelaide Branch Meeting - Brian Kirke - wave, tidal and small wind energy.

  • 2015-10-19 09:30:00
  • 34 Rosa St, Goodwood SA

When: Monday 19th Oct 2015, 7.30pm sharp

Where: Goodwood Community Centre (Banquet Hall), 32/34 Rosa St, Goodwood SA Goodwood Community Centre is convenient to the tram, buses and train. Enter the car park via Florence St.

Cost: ATA members $2. Non-members $5, tea, coffee and biscuits provided.

Presentation

Adjunct Senior Research Fellow in UniSA\'s School of Engineering and Technical Director, Seadov P/L Brian Kirke will talk about developments in wave, tidal and small wind power. These will include the Seadov floating wind and wave-powered desalination plant and other current designs of wave and tidal energy systems and their degrees of success. As well as his involvement in wave energy, Brian\'s in a partnership developing a variable pitch vertical axis wind turbine and will show how it was tested - on the back of a truck.

Q&A

ATA meetings put people with questions in touch with people who might have answers. The Q&A session is an open discussion about sustainability topics.

Meetings are open to ATA members and non-members. They aim to be informative and interactive in helping people to learn about living and building sustainability.

This is our last meeting for 2015. There will be no meeting in December or January so our next meeting will be on February 15th.
