ATA Adelaide Branch Meeting - Prof. Wasim Saman of UniSA - The Path to Low Carbon Living

  • 2015-05-18 09:30:00
  • 34 Rosa St, Goodwood SA

When: Monday 18 May 2015
Time: 7.30pm sharp
Where: Goodwood Community Centre (Banquet Hall), 32/34 Rosa St, Goodwood SA Goodwood Community Centre is convenient to the tram, buses and train. Enter the car park via Florence St.
Cost: ATA members $2. Non-members $5, Tea, coffee and biscuits provided.

Presentation: Professor Saman focusses on education and industrial research related to sustainable energy. His current projects involve solar thermal electricity generation and storage. He\'s the founding Director of the UniSA Barbara Hardy Institute and is Research Leader in the Cooperative Research Centre for Low Carbon Living.

His presentation will overview CRC activities such as the Living Lab and the Research Node for Low Carbon Living. He\'ll discuss PV with energy storage and impacts of the new electricity tariff.

Q&A

ATA meetings put people with questions in touch with people who might have answers. The Q&A session is an open discussion about sustainability topics.

Meetings are open to ATA members and non-members. They aim to be informative and interactive in helping people to learn about living and building sustainability. We look forward to seeing you.
