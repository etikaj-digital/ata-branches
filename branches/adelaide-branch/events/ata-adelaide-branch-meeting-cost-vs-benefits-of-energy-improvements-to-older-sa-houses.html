ATA Adelaide branch meeting - Cost vs benefits of energy improvements to older SA houses

  • 2018-02-19 08:30:00
  • 32 Rosa St, Goodwood SA

When: Monday 19th February 2018, 7.30pm sharp
Where: Goodwood Community Centre (Banquet Hall), 32 Rosa St, Goodwood SA. The centre is convenient to the tram, buses and train. Enter the car park via Florence St.
Cost: ATA members $2. Non-members $5, tea, coffee and biscuits provided.

Presentation:

Dr David Whaley is an electrical and electronic engineer, ATA member and UniSA academic. He researches energy efficient building techniques and teaches renewable energy technologies and their integration into existing power systems.

David will examine the costs and benefits of renovating older SA homes for energy efficiency. What energy improvements can be made, what do they cost and how effective are they? What\'s worth doing and in what order? He\'ll also describe using the ATA\'s FLIR camera to check the thermal efficiency of his own recently built house.
