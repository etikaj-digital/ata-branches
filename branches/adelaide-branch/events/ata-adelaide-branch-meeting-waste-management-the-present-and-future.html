ATA Adelaide branch meeting - Waste management - the present and future

  • 2018-08-20 09:30:00
  • 32 Rosa St, Goodwood SA

When: Monday 20 August 2018, 7.30pm
Where: Goodwood Community Centre (Banquet Hall), 32 Rosa St, Goodwood SA. The centre is convenient to the tram, buses and train. Enter the car park via Florence St.
Cost: ATA members $2. Non-members $5, tea, coffee and biscuits provided.

Presentation:

Three speakers will address this broad topic:

  • Rachael Hamilton, Coordinator, Environment and Sustainability, Campbelltown City Council
  • Tiana Nairn, Program Manager, Regulatory Reform, Environmental Protection Authority
  • Brian White, Senior Officer, Regulatory Reform, Environmental Protection Authority

China\'s limit on importing material for recycling presents a short term problem and a long term opportunity for Australia. Our speakers will address the way recyclables are currently managed and how we might handle a much wider range of material, more efficiently in the future. This includes problem streams such a e-waste, solar PV components, batteries and tyres. It will also cover the waste management hierarchy of reuse, recycling, conversion to energy and disposal.
