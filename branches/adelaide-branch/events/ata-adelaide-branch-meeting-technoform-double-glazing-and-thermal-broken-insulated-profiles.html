ATA Adelaide branch meeting - Technoform double glazing and thermal-broken insulated profiles

  • 2015-02-16 08:00:00
  • 34 Rosa St, Goodwood SA

When: Monday 16 February 2015, 7.30pm sharp
Where: Goodwood Community Centre (Banquet Hall), 32/34 Rosa St, Goodwood SA Goodwood Community Centre is convenient to the tram, buses and train. Enter the car park via Florence St.
Cost: ATA members $2. Non-members $5. Tea, coffee and biscuits provided.

Presentation: David Morehouse, Business Development Executive of Technoform-Bautec.

Technoform specialises in the manufacture of polyamide insulating profiles as used in aluminium and timber products. David has a long involvement with timber, UPVC and aluminium doors and windows especially double glazed types. He\'ll overview what\'s currently available and explain the benefits of incorporating thermal breaks into these products.

Q&A: ATA meetings put people with questions in touch with people who might have answers. The Q&A session is an open discussion about sustainability topics.

Events

Uraidla Sustainability Fair is happening again as part of the Uraidla Summertown Show on Saturday 21 February, from 9:00 am. Details at the website http://www.sustainabilityfair.com.au. It will feature demonstrations by Sophie Thomson, Harry Harrison, Kate Hubmayer, Peter Geers and Geoff Coombe. Workshops include summer pruning, children’s nature craft and making a wicking bed. If ATA members wish to promote goods or services at the fair contact admin@sustainabilityfair.com.au.

Meetings are open to ATA members and non-members. They aim to be informative and interactive in helping people to learn about living and building sustainability. We look forward to seeing you.
