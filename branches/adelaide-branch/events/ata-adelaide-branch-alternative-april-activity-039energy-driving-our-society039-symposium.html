ATA Adelaide Branch - alternative April activity: \&#039;Energy - Driving Our Society\&#039; symposium

  • 2014-04-23 08:30:00

Please note:  that there is no regular ATA meeting in April as our meeting date conflicts with Easter Monday.

The next ATA Adelaide Branch meeting will be on the 19 May 2014.

 

Energy - Driving Our Society

Sustainable Engineering Society, Engineers Australia Symposium
Framing the problem of Energy, Climate Change and Sustainability

Where: The Vines, 1 Pine Road, WOODCROFT, SA 5162 (Corner Reynell Road & Mark Street, Happy Valley)

When: Wed 23 April 2014, 8.30am to 1.30pm (optional golf 2.00pm on)

Hear from a Range of Experts and Industry Advocates:

  • Prof Gus Nathan, Adelaide Uni Centre for Energy Technology. Pathways to a clean energy future
  • David Swift, AEMO, Latest developments on the National Electricity Market
  • Chris Dalitz, Essential Energy Medium scale renewable energy integration into the grid
  • Electric Vehicles in Australia - Demonstration of an electric vehicle
  • Graham Davies, Resonant Solutions. Energy Flow in Australia – where it comes from, where it goes
  • Dr Rob Dickinson, Hydricity Systems. SA - a high economic opportunity for power-to-fuel
  • Matthew Curnow, Pangolin Assoc, Energy Efficiency – case studies in manufacturing
  • Panel discussion: Energy – Driving our Society; Steering our Future .
