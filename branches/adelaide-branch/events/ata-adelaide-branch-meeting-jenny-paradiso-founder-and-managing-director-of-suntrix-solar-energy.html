ATA Adelaide branch meeting - Jenny Paradiso, Founder and Managing Director of Suntrix Solar Energy

  • 2015-04-20 09:30:00
  • 34 Rosa St, Goodwood SA

When: Monday 20th April 2015
Time: 7.30pm sharp
Where: Goodwood Community Centre (Banquet Hall), 32/34 Rosa St, Goodwood SA Goodwood Community Centre is convenient to the tram, buses and train. Enter the car park via Florence St.
Cost: ATA members $2. Non-members $5, Tea, coffee and biscuits provided.

Presentation

Jenny Paradiso is the Founder, Managing Director and owner of SA solar company, Suntrix which she and husband David established in 2009. In its first year of operation Suntrix’s turnover grew from $250,000 to $7.3 M and over 5 years reached an annual turnover of $29 M. The company has offices in three states and over 25 employees. In the 2013 Telstra Business Awards, Suntrix was named SA\'s leading business and in Ernst and Young\'s 2014 Entrepreneur of the Year Awards, Emerging Industries category, Jenny was recognised as SA’s top entrepreneur. In 2013 and 2014 she was one of Smart Company’s Top 30 Female Entrepreneurs.

Jenny is a member of the Clean Energy Council’s (CEC) PV Leadership Committee which supports the development of renewable energy and the clean energy industry. She\'s also a founding member of the CEC’s PV Retailers\' Code of Conduct.

Jenny\'s presentation will include the following:

  • Startup and development of Suntrix including its activities, products and services.
  • Projections for future such as off/on-grid storage systems and extending existing systems.
  • Clean Energy Council\'s focus for 2015 including the Renewable Energy Target review.
  • Challenges for PV providers such as market reputation, inferior services and products and reduction of government incentives.
  • Schemes such as lease-to-buy and solar power purchase agreements.

Q&A

ATA meetings put people with questions in touch with people who might have answers. The Q&A session is an open discussion about sustainability topics.

Meetings are open to ATA members and non-members. They aim to be informative and interactive in helping people to learn about living and building sustainability. We look forward to seeing you.
