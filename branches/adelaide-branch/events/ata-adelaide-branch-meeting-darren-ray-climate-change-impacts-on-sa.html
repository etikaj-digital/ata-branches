ATA Adelaide branch meeting - Darren Ray, Climate change impacts on SA

  • 2014-07-21 09:30:00
  • 34 Rosa St, Goodwood SA

When: Monday 21 July, 7.30pm sharp
Where: Goodwood Community Centre (Banquet Hall), 32/34 Rosa St, Goodwood SA Goodwood Community Centre is convenient to the tram, buses and train. Enter the car park via Florence St.

Cost: ATA-ASC members $2. Non-members $5. Tea, coffee and biscuits provided.

Presenter: Darren Ray, SA Bureau of Meteorology.

As Senior Meteorologist/Manager of the SA Climate Section of the BOM, Darren monitors climate change and its local and wider affects. He provides climate data, seasonal forecasting, climate analysis, monitoring and communication of climate influences for South Australia.

Known for his lively style and ability to translate complex concepts into everyday language, he is regularly sought by the media to explain climate change monitoring, its effects and implications.

Darren will discuss the following:

  • Evidence of human influence on climate change
  • Natural variability and how it contributes to climate changes
  • International reaction to climate change - who did what and when
  • CSIRO-BOM project and implications for Australia
  • IPCC report and implications for the world, Australia and SA.
