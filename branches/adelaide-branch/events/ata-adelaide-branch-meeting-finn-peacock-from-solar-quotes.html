ATA Adelaide branch meeting - Finn Peacock from Solar Quotes.

  • 2016-05-16 09:30:00
  • 34 Rosa St, Goodwood SA

When: Monday 16 May 2016, 7.30pm sharp.

Where: Goodwood Community Centre (Banquet Hall), 32/34 Rosa St, Goodwood SA Goodwood Community Centre is convenient to the tram, buses and train. Enter the car park via Florence St.

Cost: ATA members $2. Non-members $5, tea, coffee and biscuits provided.

Presentation

Finn Peacock founded Solar Quotes to help people obtain the best quality quotation for a photovoltaic installation. He’ll share his experiences in adding battery storage to solar systems:

  • How battery storage systems work.
  • The four main types of storage.
  • Adding batteries to an existing system.
  • Battery ready solar systems and how to buy one.
  • How to buy a hybrid solar system.
  • Payback periods.
  • The pros and cons of going off-grid.

Q&A

ATA meetings put people with questions in touch with people who might have answers. The Q&A session is an open discussion about sustainability topics.

Meetings are open to ATA members and non-members. They aim to be informative and interactive in helping people to learn about living and building sustainability. We look forward to seeing you.
