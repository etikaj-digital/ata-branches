ATA Adelaide branch - Building monitoring for energy efficiency

  • 2018-05-21 09:30:00
  • 32 Rosa St, Goodwood SA

When: Monday 21 May 2018, 7.30pm
Where: Goodwood Community Centre (Banquet Hall), 32 Rosa St, Goodwood SA. The centre is convenient to the tram, buses and train. Enter the car park via Florence St.
Cost: ATA members $2. Non-members $5, tea, coffee and biscuits provided.

Presentation: Beth Worrall of Buddy Platform: Building monitoring for energy efficiency

Buddy Platform manufactures systems for monitoring building electrical-gas-water consumption and performance data. Although they are geared to commercial situations, Buddy\'s network-based sensors and central displays are suitable for residential applications that require more comprehensive monitoring and analysis than typical single point units provide.

Beth\'s presentation will include the following:

  • Overview of Buddy Platform\'s products and what the company does
  • Sensor types for monitoring particular parameters
  • Monitoring system case studies
  • Installation - professional or DIY
  • Overview of smart buildings and smart cities
  • Overview of Internet of Things.
