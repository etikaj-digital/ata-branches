ATA Adelaide branch - Site visit to Fluid Solar House

  • 2017-06-24 00:00:00
  • 127 Haydown Road Elizabeth Vale, SA

When: Saturday 24th June 2017,  10:00am.

Where: 127 Haydown Road Elizabeth Vale, SA. Click here for a map.

Cost: Free entry. RSVP required - see below.

RSVP: Registrations will be on a first come basis and numbers are limited so in fairness to others note that this is for members only and doesn\'t apply to partners and friends. Please quote your ATA member number when registering via adelaidebranch@ata.org.au

Fluid Solar House is a large commercial custom designed office building that uses a range of solar-thermal technologies including a heat bank, renewable energy-powered heating, cooling and ventilation system and innovative solar thermal collectors. The building\'s energy generation and management are impressive as are the products being developed inside it. These include a small turbine that\'s designed to be driven by saturated steam and suits generation using low grade waste heat.

For details of the building and products see https://fluidsolarthermal.com/technologies.html.

There may be an opportunity for ride sharing. If you\'re interested then let us know your suburb and email address when you RSVP to adelaidebranch@ata.org.au

We will then put parties in touch so they can organise this between themselves.

As always, the Committee invites suggestions for presentations and feedback regarding what they want from ATA meetings - send to adelaidebranch@ata.org.au.
