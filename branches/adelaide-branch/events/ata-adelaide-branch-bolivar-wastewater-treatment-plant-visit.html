ATA Adelaide branch - Bolivar wastewater treatment plant visit

  • 2016-05-18 00:00:00
  • Hodgson Rd, Bolivar, SA

When: Wednesday 18 May 2016, 10:00am

Where: Hodgson Rd, Bolivar, SA. Click here for a map.

Cost: Free. Open to ATA members only. See below for RSVP instructions.

Details

This fascinating sewage treatment process includes separation, gas
digestion, power generation, water recycling and processing of
biosolids. Click here for the SA Water webpage on the plants.

The tour will last 1.5 hours, all outside and include walking up and
down stairs. Visitors must wear long trousers, long sleeves and closed
shoes. Eye protection will be provided.

Book via adelaidebranch@ata.org.au including your name and membership number.
It\'s limited to 30 people and is first-come-first-served. If there\'s a
large response we\'ll try to organise another tour. We\'ll notify all
respondents whether they are in the group for the 18th May or on a waiting
list.

This is open to ATA members only - sorry, no non-member family or
friends.

The plant is on Hodgson Road at Bolivar approx 20 km north of the city.
We\'ll try to put those who want to car pool in contact with others in
their direction. If so please indicate which part of Adelaide you live
in (North, South, East, West) and we\'ll forward your email address to
others in your area so you can make contact.
