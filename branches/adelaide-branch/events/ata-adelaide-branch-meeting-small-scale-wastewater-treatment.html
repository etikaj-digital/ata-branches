ATA Adelaide branch meeting - Small scale wastewater treatment.

  • 2016-03-21 08:30:00
  • 34 Rosa St, Goodwood SA

When: Monday 21 March 2016, 7.30pm sharp.

Where: Goodwood Community Centre (Banquet Hall), 32/34 Rosa St, Goodwood SA Goodwood Community Centre is convenient to the tram, buses and train. Enter the car park via Florence St.

Cost: ATA members $2. Non-members $5, tea, coffee and biscuits provided.

Presentation

Via his company Wastewater Engineering, Philip Mercer designs treatment plants for small communities. His presentation will overview the history of water treatment in Australia, how treatment plants operate and issues related to small scale blackwater and greywater treatment. He’ll also discuss guidelines and regulations for water treatment and reuse.

Visit to Bolivar

There was a good response to last month’s call for expressions of interest in a tour of the Bolivar Sewage Treatment Plant. If demand exceeds the maximum 30 places, we’ll organise a second tour.

Q&A

ATA meetings put people with questions in touch with people who might have answers. The Q&A session is an open discussion about sustainability topics.

Meetings are open to ATA members and non-members. They aim to be informative and interactive in helping people to learn about living and building sustainability. We look forward to seeing you.
