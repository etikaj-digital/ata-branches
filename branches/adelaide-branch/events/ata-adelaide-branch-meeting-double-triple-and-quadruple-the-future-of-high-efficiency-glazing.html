ATA Adelaide Branch Meeting - Double, triple and quadruple - the future of high efficiency glazing

  • 2015-09-21 09:30:00
  • 34 Rosa St, Goodwood SA

When: Monday 21 September 2015, 7.30pm sharp
Where: Goodwood Community Centre (Banquet Hall), 32/34 Rosa St, Goodwood SA Goodwood Community Centre is convenient to the tram, buses and train. Enter the car park via Florence St.
Cost: ATA members $2. Non-members $5, Tea, coffee and biscuits provided.

Presentation: 

Alicja Matejko, MD of Oknalux will discuss the construction and performance of their range of European made, highly energy efficient windows. She\'ll compare Europe\'s manufacturing standards and market with Australia\'s and explain the involved process of testing and certification that Australian importers must follow to ensure compliance.

Oknalux supplies double, triple and even quadruple glazed units with frames of uPVC, aluminium and timber and glazing to suit different applications. Alicja will explain how frames and glass have developed in recent years to improve performance and lifespan.

The manufacturer builds 7,000 units a week and was awarded European Medals for innovation and quality.
