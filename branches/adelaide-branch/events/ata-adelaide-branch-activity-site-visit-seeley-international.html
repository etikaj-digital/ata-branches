ATA Adelaide branch activity - Site visit - Seeley International

  • 2017-04-05 06:45:00
  • 112 O’Sullivan Beach Road, Lonsdale, SA

When: Wednesday 5th April 2017,  5:45pm assemble in car park for 6:00 pm start

Where: 112 O’Sullivan Beach Road, Lonsdale

Cost: Free entry. Booking required, but current ATA members only please - quote member number with booking.

Dress: Closed toe shoes. If you have your own high visibility vest please bring it along.

Seeley International Pty Ltd - Energy efficient air cooling

Some months ago we had a presentation by Frank Seeley about his company’s highly energy efficient Climate Wizard air cooling system. He invited us to visit the factory to see the system in production and if we’re lucky, under test.

There was a call for expressions of interest but even if you responded to that please now book via adelaidebranch@ata.org.au including your membership number. We’ll make every effort to include all who wish to attend on the 5th April but if it’s not possible we’ll try to arrange another visit at a future date.

Branch committee election

Nominations by currently financial members are invited for the ATA Adelaide Branch Committee – Convenor, Treasurer and General Committee Member. Please notify your wish to nominate via adelaidebranch@ata.org.au and the election will be held at the May 15th meeting.

As always, the Committee invites suggestions for presentations and feedback regarding what they want from ATA meetings - send to adelaidebranch@ata.org.au.
