ATA Adelaide branch meeting - Electric vehicle charging infrastructure

  • 2017-11-20 08:30:00
  • 32 Rosa St, Goodwood SA

When: Monday 20 NovemberÂ 2017, 7.30pm sharp
Where: Goodwood Community Centre (Banquet Hall), 32 Rosa St, Goodwood SA. The centre is convenient to the tram, buses and train. Enter the car park via Florence St.
Cost: ATA members $2. Non-members $5, tea, coffee and biscuits provided.

Presentation:
GELCO Services specialises in electric vehicle charging systems, battery monitoring and infrastructure. CEO Bob Gell will explain the types of charging station, how they operate and applications. He\'ll review the progress of charging infrastructure in Australia in readiness for long distance travel. Bob has just completed an extensive study trip across Europe to examine support infrastructure. This included chairing sessions of the EV Symposium in Stuttgart.

Annual meeting and election of officers
February 19th 2018 will be our annual meeting with the election of Convenor, Treasurer and Committee for 2018. Nominations are invited via adelaidebranch@ata.org.au.

Visit to Peats Soil
Expressions of interest are invited for a visit to Peats Soil at Brinkley (near Langhorne Creek) on a Saturday in March 2018. Features include organic waste conversion, a biodiesel plant and the BiobiN, a material processing plant made by Peats and sold internationally.
This notice is to gauge interest and bookings will be invited closer to the time. Car pooling will be organised. Please note members only: adelaidebranch@ata.org.au.
