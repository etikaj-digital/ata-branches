ATA Adelaide Branch meeting - May 19 - Hydronic Heating

  • 2014-05-19 19:30:00
  • 34 Rosa St, Goodwood SA

When: Monday 19 May 2014

Time: 7.30pm sharp

Where: Goodwood Community Centre (Banquet Hall), 32/34 Rosa St, Goodwood SA (Goodwood Community Centre is convenient to the tram (stop 3), buses and train. Enter the car park via Florence St.). Click here for a map of the venue.

Entry fee: ATA members $2. Non-members $5. Tea, coffee and biscuits provided.

Presenter: Len Finney of GGS Hydronic Heating, hydronic system designer and constructor.

Through his company Guardian Gas Services, Len has a long experience in the design and installation of in-floor heating and other types of water-based systems. He installed the underfloor system at the St Peters\' house, open for Sustainable House Day 2013.

Len will discuss the following:

  • What\'s meant by hydronic heating
  • Performance compared to other forms of space heating
  • Cost benefits
  • Heat sources - gas or solar
  • Use in suspended floors or timber floors
  • Use for cooling
  • Problems - pre heating overnight then the next day is warm
  • Maintenance
  • Retrofitting in existing buildings.

Q&A session

ATA meetings put people with questions in touch with people who might have answers. The Q&A session is an open discussion about sustainability topics. For example:

  • Who can help with an issue? If you\'d prefer, put the query on a slip of paper and give to one of the committee before the meeting.
  • What\'s on in other parts of Adelaide, SA and Australia?
  • Any events you\'ve been to and what you\'ve learned.
