Contact the branch at adelaidebranch@ata.org.au

The Renew Adelaide Branch meetings are very informative and regularly have 50-60 attendees. So come along to see and hear about the latest and greatest in sustainability at 7.30pm on the 3rd Monday of the month at the Goodwood Community Centre, 32-34 Rosa St, Goodwood.

Please note: There will be no meeting in September or October and our next will be on 19 November 2018.
