Melbourne branch

  • Overview
  • Events

The Renew Melbourne Branch provides a forum for people in the Melbourne region to share their interest in sustainability, including renewable energy, conserving energy, good building design and water conservation. These topics often have a practical slant and include members’ hands-on experience.

Meetings

Usually held monthly on the third Wednesday of the month from February to November. Please note the specific venue location and time for each event. Meetings are generally held at Swinburne University Hawthorn or The Energy Transition Hub (Melb Uni) in Carlton, both commencing at 7pm.

The main focus of a meeting is a presentation by one or more guest speakers. There is plenty of opportunity for asking general questions to the group and socialising after the formal presentation.

There is normally no need to rsvp unless noted on the event page. Entry is free, but a gold coin donation is appreciated to help offset costs, and visitors are always very welcome at our events.
