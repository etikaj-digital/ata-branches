Melbourne ATA Branch Meeting - Film Night: The Future of Energy

  • 2014-09-17 09:00:00
  • 397 Burwood Rd, Hawthorn VIC 3122

When: 17 September, 2014 at 7:00 pm

Where: Swinburne University of Technology, Hawthorn Campus (Engineering Building – Room EN615 - note room change). Click here for directions.

Entry: Gold Coin Donation to cover costs.

The Future of Energy
This film by Brett Mazurek, Maximilian DeArmon, Missy Lahren & Theo Badashi was shown at the Transitions Film Festival in Melbourne on 21 February this year and received a great response. We are pleased to be able to present it at our September meeting. More details of the film are on the Transitions Film Festival website here and the film website itself is here.

A summary of the film taken from the website says "The Future of Energy: Lateral Power to the People is a positive, fun film that focuses on energy solutions and the people behind the renewable energy revolution. It\'s a love story about people re-imagining their relationship to the planet, and falling back in love with the Earth and each other."
The film runs for 1 hr 15 min. which leaves time for a discussion about issues raised by the film and informal conversations over tea/coffee following the movie (the latter subject to facilities availability).
This is a great opportunity to see a film that is unlikely to be widely distributed. We have been offered a theatre-style lecture room for this meeting with a capacity of 160, so please feel free to invite guests to this meeting!

Convenor: Peter Wonfor
Followed by discussion and refreshments
