ATA Melbourne Branch - Biodiesel Workshop

  • 2014-10-18 21:30:00
  • Cnr Roberts and Stewart Streets, Brunswick East, Victoria

Where: Multicultural Classroom, CERES, Cnr Roberts and Stewart Streets, East Brunswick.  Maps here.

When: Sunday 19 October, 8:30 am ~ 1:00 pm

Cost: $10 (Maximum 20 people)

RSVP essential: email to ata.melbourne.branch@gmail.com

Workshop Details:

The workshop will be given by Jonathan Thwaites.  Jonathan is an ATA Member and Biodiesel enthusiast and among other interesting things has done many biodiesel workshops for the University of Western Australia.  Johathan will be making a small demonstration batch of biodiesel in the course of the workshop.  Biodiesel can be made from many "waste product" feedstocks such as used cooking oils, tallow and fats from meat processors.  Jonathan will be using used cooking oil at this workshop; this is the feedstock most likely to be available to biodiesel home-brewers.

As a result of this cost reduction we anticipate the workshop could become over-subscribed, so an RSVP is essential to be sure of a place.  Please RSVP only if you wish to attend.

Event convenor; Peter Wonfor.
