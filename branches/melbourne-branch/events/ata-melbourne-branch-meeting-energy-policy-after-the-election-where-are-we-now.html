ATA Melbourne branch meeting - Energy Policy After The Election: Where Are We Now?

  • 2016-07-20 09:00:00
  • 397 Burwood Rd, Hawthorn VIC 3122

When: Wednesday 20 July, 2016, 6.45 for 7:00pm

Where: Engineering (EN) Building, Swinburne University, Hawthorn Campus, Room EN615 (if you are unfamiliar with this location view or download this Hawthorn campus map).

Entry: Free.  A gold coin donation to offset costs would be appreciated.

Presenter: Dean Lombard, ATA\'s Senior Energy Analyst

Bio: As Senior Energy Analyst at the ATA, Dean Lombard has his finger on the pulse of the Australia\'s energy system and market. He recently joined the ATA with a significant background in energy policy having worked in a similar role for the Victorian Council of Social Service (VCOSS) for 12 years where he focused primarily on energy policies and frameworks for low income and disadvantaged consumers.

Presentation: Among other topics relevant to energy following the recent election Dean will include Smart Meters, bi-directional distribution networks and possible changes to the National Energy Market (NEM).

We anticipate yet another interesting Q&A session following Dean\'s presentation.

Regards, Chris and the Melbourne branch committee.
