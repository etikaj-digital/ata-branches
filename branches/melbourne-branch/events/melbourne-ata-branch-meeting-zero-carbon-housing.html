Melbourne ATA Branch Meeting - Zero Carbon Housing

  • 2014-11-19 08:00:00
  • Engineering Building, Swinburne University, 397 Burwood Rd, Hawthorn VIC 3122

Where: Swinburne University of Technology, Hawthorn Campus (Engineering Building – Room EN615). Click here for directions.

When: Wednesday 19 November, 2014 at 7:00 pm

Cost: Gold Coin Donation to cover costs.
Mr Michael Ambrose of CSIROwill give a talk on the Zero Emission House Project. This house produces enough energy to satisfy its needs. This ZEH house has been designed specifically to be sold into the volume housing market by meeting the design requirements and budget of a typical middle income Australian family.

He will discuss the findings and how the assumptions of the project matched or didn\'t match the real world and further research to drive the public to change their behavior to make Australian homes more energy efficient.
More information is available here.

Followed by Q and A, discussion and refreshments

Meeting convenor: Vish Karegowda
