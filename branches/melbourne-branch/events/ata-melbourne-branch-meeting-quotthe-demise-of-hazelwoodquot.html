ATA Melbourne branch meeting: \&quot;The Demise of Hazelwood?\&quot;

  • 2016-02-17 08:00:00
  • 397 Burwood Rd, Hawthorn VIC 3122
When: Wednesday 17 February 2016, 6.30 for 7:00pm
Where: Engineering (EN) Building, Swinburne University, Hawthorn Campus,  Room EN615. If you have trouble locating the Engineering Building (EN) then you look at this Hawthorn campus map.
Entry: Gold Coin Donation to cover costs.
Presentation: Robin Gardner
Hazelwood is the dirtiest generator in Australia and one of the dirtiest in the world as well as being the oldest large generator in Australia.  So will it be one of the first coal-fired power plants to be  decommissioned as a result of climate change mitigation? This would be the sensible step, following on from the Paris conference. Especially as renewable energy sources prove themselves elsewhere to be practical energy providers.
Rob has done extensive research into the history of Hazelwood and other dirty generators (and the power system that they are a part of) and has observations and conclusions that are both interesting and provocative. So please come and find out why this is wishful thinking and what the reality is likely to be.
