ATA Melbourne branch event - ATA current, future and energy policy update

  • 2017-07-19 09:00:00
  • 397 Burwood Rd, Hawthorn VIC 3122

When: Wednesday 19 July 2017, 6.45 for 7:00pm
Where: Engineering (EN) Building, Swinburne University, Hawthorn Campus, Room EN101 (if you are unfamiliar with this location view or download this Hawthorn campus map).
Cost: Free. (A gold coin donation would be appreciated to offset costs.)

Presenters:   Donna Luckman and Andrew Reddaway

Come along to the next Melbourne branch meeting to hear Donna Luckman, ATA\'s CEO, discuss the organisations\' current activities and bold vision for a changing future and how ATA’s leadership on practical action against climate change is making an impact. Andrew Reddaway, ATA’s Energy Analyst will also present on how big-picture energy policy connects with what people can do in their own home. Th meeting will also include updates on ATA’s latest research on battery storage, switching from gas and a new free online solar/battery calculator to be released soon.
