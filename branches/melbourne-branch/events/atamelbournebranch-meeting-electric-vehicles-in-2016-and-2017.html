ATA Melbourne Branch Meeting - Electric Vehicles in 2016 and 2017

  • 2016-08-17 09:00:00
  • 397 Burwood Rd, Hawthorn VIC 3122

When: Wednesday 17 August, 2016, 6.45 for 7:00pm

Where: NEW VENUE - Augustine Centre/Habitat Uniting Church, 2 Minona Street, Hawthorn. Click here for a map.

Entry: Free.  A gold coin donation to offset costs would be appreciated.

Presenter: Paul Paton, Convenor, ATA Melbourne EV branch.

Presentation:

To say EVs are coming of age is probably an understatement - if not here and now for the majority certainly for the early adopters and those with a good match between driving habits and the availability of renewable energy (especially the home-generated variety).

Paul will give us an update not only on the technological advances but also on vehicles available in 2016 and 2017

Paul Paton has spent a long time researching developments in EV’s, He has modified Vectrix electric scooters for Li-Ion battery upgrades. to give 120 – 350 km range, constructed batteries, battery management systems and power electronics for Electric Vehicles and constructed electronic equipment including audio, test, lighting and microprocessor controlled projects.
