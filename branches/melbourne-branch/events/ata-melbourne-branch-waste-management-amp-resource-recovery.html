ATA Melbourne branch - Waste Management &amp; Resource Recovery

  • 2015-10-21 08:00:00
  • 397 Burwood Rd, Hawthorn VIC 3122

When: Wednesday 21 October 2015, 6.30 for 7:00pm

Where: Swinburne University, Hawthorn Campus,  Building BA (Business & Arts)  Room 302

Where:  Swinburne University of Technology, Hawthorn Campus, Room BA302. NOTE: This meeting is not in the usual Engineering building…(Business & Arts Building, opposite the Library across the Atrium. Refer to grid reference G5 on the Swinburne campus map here).

Entry: Gold Coin Donation to cover costs.

Presentation: We all generate waste and rely on waste management services to deal with the result.  Increasingly, waste materials are being seen as a resource.  The October Melbourne Branch meeting brings together three speakers working in different areas of waste management and resource recovery:

James Depasquale from Whittlesea City Council will give the municipal perspective and talk about community capacity building and behaviour change projects to divert waste from landfill.

Jaye Muir of Suez (formerly Sita), which collects and processes municipal and commercial waste, will talk about its large-scale operations.  Interested groups can arrange a tour of the Hampton Park facility.

Vanessa Petrie and Shannon Smyth of the Metropolitan Waste Management and Resource Recovery Group will provide an overview and future directions of the Metropolitan WM&RR Implementation Plan.

The presentation will be followed by Q and A and informal discussion.

We hope to see you there,

Ro and the Melbourne branch committee.
