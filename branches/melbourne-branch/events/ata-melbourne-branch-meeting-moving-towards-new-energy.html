ATA Melbourne branch meeting - Moving Towards New Energy

  • 2017-03-15 08:00:00
  • 50 Park St, Hawthorn, VIC

When: Wednesday 15 March 2017, 6.45 for 7:00pm
Where: TD Building, Swinburne University, Hawthorn Campus, Room TD121. Note the change of building. If you are unfamiliar with this location view or download this Hawthorn campus map. There is parking in Park St, William St, and on the Glenferrie Rd side of the building. If travelling by train then take the same exit from the station, but continue along the walkway until you reach John St. Turn left and walk up towards Park St and the TD building is on your left.
Cost: Free. (A gold coin donation would be appreciated to offset costs.)

Presenter: Travis Hughes, AGL\'s head of market and product development, new energy

At the next meeting of the Alternative Technology Association\'s (ATA) Melbourne Branch, Travis Hughes will speak about the challenges presented to the energy industry by the transition to renewable energy, including variable power levels and distributed generation.

This will be a fascinating topic that will inform us about the practical difficulties 21st-century energy generation places in a complex distribution system built in the last century to work with centralised fossil-fuel technology.

Please feel free to invite friends and colleagues - our meetings are open to ATA members and guests. We look forward to seeing you there!
