ATA Melbourne branch - Site Visit - Cranbourne Botanic Gardens guided tour

  • 2016-11-19 23:00:00
  • Corner Ballarto Road and Botanic Drive, Cranbourne VIC 3977

When: Sunday 20 November 2016, 10.00-11.00 am

Where: Botanic Gardens, Corner Ballarto Road and Botanic Drive, Cranbourne VIC 3977. Click here for a map.

Bookings: Eventbrite details here. BOOKED OUT

Join the ATA Melbourne branch for a walking tour of the Australian Garden (Royal Botanic Gardens, Cranbourne), with guide Warren Warboys, the Botanic Gardens Director. Warren is very knowledgeable and will feature "water saving and minimising usage" as a main topic and "the garden\'s use of ground source heat pumps" as a secondary topic, and is willing to discuss all range of topics.

You will be able to stay on for lunch at the gardens, either in the cafe which offers a range of nice, reasonably priced choices or BYO. You may also join a group who is planing on walking further around the gardens after lunch.
