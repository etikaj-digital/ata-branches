Melbourne EV Branch

  • Overview
  • Events

This branch is to provide a forum for anyone interested in Electric Vehicles such as bicycles, cars and other forms of transport.

Organisers of[Melbourne EV Expo]](http://www.evexpo.org.au)

Meetings

Meetings are held on the 4th Wednesday of each month at 7:00pm in the Engineering Faculty, Swinburne University, Hawthorn campus (entry via the EN building).

For a map of Swinburne see http://www.swinburne.edu.au/campuses/hawthorn/index.html

Format

There is a monthly 30-45 minute meeting where matters pertaining to electric vehicles are discussed followed by a guest speaker who speaks to one of the many topics relating to electric vehicles. The formal part of the night is followed by an informal gathering for tea, coffee and biscuits and a chance to catch up with like minded people. There is no need to book and everyone (Renew member or not) is most welcome.

You can see some of our previous meetings on our Youtube channel.

You can use Google+ to connect to ask questions before and during the meeting (currently being tested).
