ATA Melbourne EV branch - Christmas Breakup

  • 2016-12-03 00:00:00
  • Finns Reserve Picnic Area, Duncan St, Templestowe Lower VIC 3107

When: Saturday 3 Dec 2016, 11:00am

Where: Finns Reserve Picnic Area, Duncan St, Templestowe. VIC. Click here for a map.

Join us for lunch this Saturday to celebrate the end of another year and ever increasing presence of electric vehicles. We will joined by members of the Australian Electric Vehicle Association (AEVA) and anyone interested in EVs is welcome to come along and have a chat over lunch.

We will be having lunch at Finns Reserve Picnic Area next to the Yarra River and Wombat Bend. Electric barbecues and covered seating will be available, BYO everything else.
