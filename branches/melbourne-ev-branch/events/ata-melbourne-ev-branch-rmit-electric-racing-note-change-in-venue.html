ATA Melbourne EV branch - RMIT Electric Racing (note change in venue)

  • 2014-09-24 09:00:00
  • 445 Swanston St, Melbourne VIC 3000
When: Wednesday 24 September 2014, 7:00pm
Where: RMIT, City campus, Building 80, Room 80.02.07, Enter via Swanston St, Map + get directions
Please note this meeting will be at RMIT university.

Professor Simon Watkins and members of RMIT Electric Racing will present on their electric racing ventures including specifics about this years and last years racing car.

There will be an opportunity to see there car at the meeting. You can find more information about RMIT Electric Racing at electric.rmitracing.com
