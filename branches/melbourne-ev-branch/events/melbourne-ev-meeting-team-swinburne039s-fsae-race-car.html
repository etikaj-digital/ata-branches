Melbourne EV meeting - Team Swinburne\&#039;s FSAE Race Car

  • 2014-10-29 08:00:00
  • 397 Burwood road, Hawthorn VIC 3122

When: Wed 29 October 2014, 7:00pm

Where: Swinburne University, Hawthorn campus, Room EN615. For a map of Swinburne click here.

Team Swinburne will present the design and implementation of their race car.

They are dedicated team of Engineering and Business students who design, build, market and race Swinburne\'s electric Formula SAE car against other Universities from across the Asia-Pacific region. The competition takes place at Victoria University\'s Werribee campus, during mid-December.

Find out more about the team at teamswinburne.com and www.facebook.com/teamswinburne
