ATA Melbourne EV branch meeting - A Beginner\&#039;s Guide to Electric Vehicles on Sale in Australia

  • 2018-05-23 09:00:00
  • 397 Burwood road, Hawthorn VIC 3122

When: Wednesday 23 May 2017, 7:00pm
Where: Room EN515, Engineering Building (EN), Swinburne Hawthorn Campus. If you have trouble locating the Engineering Building (EN) then you look at this Hawthorn campus map.

Presentation: A Beginner\'s Guide to Electric Vehicles on Sale in Australia - BEV, PHEV,HEV, FCEV

Bryce Gaton will be our presenter. The session will describe the motor/engine used in each model and the fuel/s consumed. He will emphasise the advantages and disadvantages of each technology and show how to evaluate which is the right one for your needs"

Also "News" of Electric Vehicles Australia and Worldwide

Bryce has been working in the EV sector for 10 years building and maintaining converted EVs, and currently works part-time for Melbourne University\'s School of Engineering as their EV safe work practice trainer/supervisor. He regularly writes on EV topics for ReNew magazine as well as presenting about them on behalf of both the AEVA and ATA.

Members of the Melbourne Branch of the AEVA are being invited to attend.
