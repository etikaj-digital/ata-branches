Renew Melbourne EV Branch meeting - Looking after your EV battery

  • 2018-09-26 09:00:00
  • 397 Burwood road, Hawthorn VIC 3122

When: Wednesday 26 September 2017, 7:00pm
Where: Room TBA, Engineering Building (EN), Swinburne Hawthorn Campus. Follow the signs to the room. If you have trouble locating the Engineering Building (EN) then you look at this Hawthorn campus map.

Presentation: Looking after your EV battery

Bryce Gaton will present on the basics and care of EV batteries and how to monitor Nissan Leaf and Mistubishi iMiEV batteries with aftermarket apps, using the 2011 – 2017 Nissan Leaf as an example (this is particularly relevant if you are thinking of buying a second hand electric vehicle).

Bryce has been working in the EV sector for 10 years building and maintaining converted EVs, and currently works part-time for Melbourne University\'s School of Engineering as their EV safe work practice trainer/supervisor. He regularly writes on EV topics for Renew magazine plus the websites ‘TheDriven’ and Renew Economy. He also regularly presents about them on behalf of both AEVA and Renew.

  •  This month\'s meeting will be chaired by Kevin Woodhouse. As part of the "News" section Kevin will talk about charging facilities proposed for Victoria.
  • Members of the Melbourne Branch of the AEVA are being invited to attend.

We hope to see you at the meeting.
