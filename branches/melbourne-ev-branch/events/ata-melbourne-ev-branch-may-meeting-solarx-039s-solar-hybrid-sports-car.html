ATA Melbourne EV Branch May meeting - SolarX \&#039;s Solar Hybrid Sports Car

  • 2014-05-28 19:00:00
  • 397 Burwood road, Hawthorn VIC 3122

When: Wednesday 28 May 2014
Time: 7:00pm
Where: The meeting will be held in the Engineering Building (EN) of Swinburne University, Hawthorn campus. Room EN615. For a map of Swinburne click here.

Presenter: SolarX Corporation is pioneering a solar hybrid road legal car. This project is supported by the Aurora Solar Car Team and Swinburne University Mechanical Engineering. SolarX Corporation\'s aim is to bring new technologies to market while developing projects like the Solar Hybrid Sports Car. SolarX was profiled in the last issue of the ATA\'s ReNew magazine.

Follow the journey from idea to prototype ready to build. Some of the technologies trialed include regenerative shock absorbers for recovering power and the ability to integrate the solar panels with home electricity use to offset electricity use while the car is parked. The car features a hybrid battery design using both Li-Ion batteries coupled with supercapacitors to improve acceleration and battery life. They will also talk about how they are marketing and raising venture capital for implementing a prototype car.
