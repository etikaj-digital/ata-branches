ATA Melbourne EV branch meeting - The work and vision of BZE (Beyond Zero Emissions)

  • 2014-07-30 09:00:00
  • 397 Burwood road, Hawthorn VIC 3122

When: Wednesday 30 July 2014, 7:00pm
Where: Swinburne University, Hawthorn campus, Room EN615. For a map of Swinburne click here.

Presenter: Dr Stephen Bygrave, the CEO of Beyond Zero Emissions. He will be talking about BZE\'s work and vision, including their research into electric vehicles and high speed rail.

Stephen has worked on renewable energy, energy efficiency, sustainable transport, emissions trading and climate change for over 20 years. He has worked on village scale renewable energy projects in Fiji, Solomon Islands and Kiribati, as a senior executive in the Australian government, and on international climate policy with the OECD in Paris, France. Stephen worked on the design of Australia’s mandatory renewable energy target in the late 1990s, as well as on Australia’s clean energy future package.

Beyond Zero Emissions (BZE) is a climate change think tank based in Melbourne, Australia. Its goal is to transform Australia from a 19th century fossil fuel based, emissions intensive economy to a 21st-century renewable-energy-powered clean-tech economy.
