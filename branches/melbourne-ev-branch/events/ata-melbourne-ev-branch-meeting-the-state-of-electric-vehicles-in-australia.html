ATA Melbourne EV branch meeting - The State of Electric Vehicles in Australia

  • 2017-07-26 09:00:00
  • 397 Burwood road, Hawthorn VIC 3122

When: Wednesday 26 July 2017, 7:00pm

Where: Engineering Building (EN), Swinburne University, Hawthorn campus, Burwood road, Hawthorn. Follow the signs to Room EN101. If you have trouble locating the Engineering Building (EN) then you look at this Hawthorn campus map.

Presenter: Claire Painter, ClimateWorks Australia

ClimateWorks Australia has prepared The State of Electric Vehicles in Australia report for The Electric Vehicle Council of Australia. This report provides an insight into what is required to encourage mass uptake of electric vehicles in Australia by examining key elements of the current state of play, including:

  • Consumer attitudes
  • Government policy
  • Charging infrastructure
  • Electric Vehicle uptake

Other EV News will be discussed including

  • Federal minister announces Electric Vehicle Council Launch May 2017
  • Australia Post Electric vehicle trial
  • Tesla Model 3 first production models
  • 100MW Tesla storage battery for South Australia

We hope to see you at the meeting,
