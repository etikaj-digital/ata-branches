ATA Melbourne EV branch - The Impact of Electric Vehicles on the Grid

  • 2015-10-28 08:00:00
  • 397 Burwood road, Hawthorn VIC 3122

When: Wednesday 28 October 2015, 7:00pm

Where: Swinburne University, Hawthorn campus, Burwood road, Hawthorn (follow signs to room). If you have trouble locating the Engineering Building (EN) then you look at this Hawthorn campus map.

Presentation: Julian de Hoog is a Research Staff Member at IBM Research - Australia, working on a variety of projects in renewables, storage, and electrified transport. Julian was formerly a Research Fellow at the University of Melbourne where he spent three years working on a project exploring the impact of electric vehicles on the grid, and proposing solutions to time-shift EV charging. The research project was conducted in conjunction with the Victorian Department of Transport’s Electric Vehicle Trial. Julian will present on the project\'s key outcomes.
