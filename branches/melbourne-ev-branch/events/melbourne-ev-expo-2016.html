Melbourne EV Expo 2016

  • 2016-10-16 00:00:00
  • 397 Burwood road, Hawthorn VIC 3122

What is the Electric Vehicle Expo?

The Expo will be a one-day event presented jointly by the Alternative Technology Association, Swinburne University and Boroondara Council. The expo will be a family friendly, relaxed and informative day exploring and showcasing Melbourne’s electric transport options, with a particular emphasis on electric bicycles.

When: Sunday 16 October 2016

Time: 10:00am – 4:00pm

Where: ATC Building, Swinburne University, Hawthorn Campus. Click here for a map. Click here for a campus map.

The Expo will include up to 30 stalls, demonstrations, speakers and panel discussions relating to all modes of electric transport.

There will also be a “Show & Shine” display where you can show off your electric bike, car, motorbike or scooter (or other electric vehicle). There will be prizes for the Most Attractive Vehicle, Best Workmanship,  (conversions only) and Best Concept. You can see an example of the Show & Shine scorecard here. Please note the Show & Shine display is not for commercial promotion. If you have a business product that you would like to showcase then please use the ‘Stall Holder’ entry form.
