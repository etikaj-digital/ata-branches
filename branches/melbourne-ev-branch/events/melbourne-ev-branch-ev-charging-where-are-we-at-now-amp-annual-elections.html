Melbourne EV Branch - “EV Charging, where are we at now?” (&amp; annual elections)

  • 2013-11-27 19:00:00
  • 397 Burwood road, Hawthorn VIC 3122, Australia

The presentation will be preceded by our annual elections(should be quite brief), where there are a couple of positions on the committee which have become available, so please consider putting your hand up to help the group continue to operate.

 

Presenter : Riccardo Pagliarella

Where: The meeting will be held in the Engineering Building, in room EN615, at Swinburne University, Hawthorn campus. For a map of Swinburne click here.

Cost: Gold coin donation toward tea/coffee.

Riccardo is a Mechanical Engineer and Aerodynamicist.  He is currently standing as a Committee member of Standards Australia, setting standards for EV charging.  He also holds a position as Environment Policy Manager at Toyota Motor Corporation.

Previously Riccardo was a Design Engineer for Tesla Motors, & an Aerodynamicist for the Renault F1 racing team.

Education :  PhD in Vehicle Aerodynamics At RMIT, Bachelor of Engineering with Honours at University of Melbourne. Bachelor of Commerce at University of Melbourne

We anticipate an interesting discussion on the state of the EV industry in Australia, and the emerging standards for charging infrastructure.

We suggest you forward this invitation to the Sustainability Officer or similar at your local council, and any other networks you think may appreciate learning about the future of EV infrastructure.

For those interested in sharing dinner beforehand from 6pm, several of us meet informally at a curry cafe across the road at 424 Burwood Rd, formally called Funky Curry, now called Only Vegetarian Kitchen (which is not strictly true, they cater to non-vego\'s too!)..  Check out details here.

Also, if you would like to attend the Formula SAE-A comp in Werribee in a couple of weeks, we plan to have a bit of a Christmas break-up gathering there on December 15th.. Keep your eye out for details.

We look forward to seeing you soon!

Thanks & Regards.
