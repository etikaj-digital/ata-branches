Formula SAE Race Event and EV branch Christmas get together

  • 2013-12-15 12:30:00
  • 4 Hoppers Ln Werribee VIC 3030
On a more social note, we are arranging an end-of-year get together at the Formula SAE-A event in Werribee on Sunday December 15th.  We will have a marquee set up for our members, and anyone who wants to bring their EV is most welcome (but cars must arrive by 8am, to get them on display alongside our marquee).
If you would like to come, please let us know so we can keep you informed of updates.  And please BYO chair and nibbles to share.

Details of the SAE event can be found here. There are a number of pure-EV entries.

I\'m sure the weather will be amazing.  See you all there.
