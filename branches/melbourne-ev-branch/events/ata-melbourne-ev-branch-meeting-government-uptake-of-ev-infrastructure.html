ATA Melbourne EV branch meeting - Government uptake of EV Infrastructure

  • 2014-03-26 19:00:00
  • 397 Burwood road, Hawthorn VIC 3122, Australia
Where: Engineering Building, Room EN615, at Swinburne University, Hawthorn campus. For a map of Swinburne click here.
Cost: Gold coin donation toward tea/coffee.
Register (optional) at:  https://www.facebook.com/events/615153845221316/
Presenter : Stuart Nesbitt

Stuart Nesbitt has been the "Climate Change Technical Officer" at Moreland City Council from June 2010 to present. He was trained in Renewable Energy Technology by Swinburne University.

Stuart will talk about Council implementation of EV charging networks, and also about his own experience with electric vehicles. He recently worked on implementation of an EV charging network through Moreland, including a fast charging station at the Moreland City Council funded by the state government costing $38,000.

Stuart is also an EV hobbyist who has worked on several electric vehicles including a long range Vectrix conversion capable of over 200 Kms.
