ATA Melbourne EV branch - Christmas Breakup with AEVA

  • 2017-11-25 23:00:00
  • Duncan St, Templestowe Lower VIC 3107

When: Sunday 26 November 2017, 10:00am - 3:00pm
Where: Finns Reserve Picnic Area, Duncan St, Templestowe. Click here for a Google map.

Join us for lunch this Sunday to celebrate the end of a big year electric vehicles. As last year, we will joined by members of the Australian Electric Vehicle Association (AEVA). Anyone interested in EVs is welcome to come along and meet the new branch committee members for both the ATA Melbourne EV Branch and AEVA Melbourne Branch or just to have a chat over lunch.

We will be having lunch at Finns Reserve Picnic Area next to the Yarra River and Wombat Bend. Electric barbecues and covered seating will be available, BYO everything else.

See you there,
