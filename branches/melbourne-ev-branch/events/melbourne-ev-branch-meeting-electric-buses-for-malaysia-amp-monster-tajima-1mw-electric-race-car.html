Melbourne EV branch meeting - Electric Buses for Malaysia &amp; Monster Tajima 1MW Electric Race Car

  • 2015-06-24 09:00:00
  • 397 Burwood road, Hawthorn VIC 3122

When: Wednesday 24 June 2015, 7:00pm
Where: Swinburne University, Hawthorn campus, Burwood road, Hawthorn, Room EN203. If you have trouble locating the Engineering Building (EN) then you look at this Hawthorn campus map.

Electric Buses for the Malaysia Automotive Institute
Jason Miller from Swinburne University will present on Swinburne\'s work to develop technologies for an Electric Bus for Malaysia. Swinburne University, the Malaysia Automotive Institute (MAI) and AutoCRC are working together to develop technologies for electrified buses to service the public transport system in Malaysia. Jason Miller is the Research Manager (Transport Theme) for the Centre for Sustainable Infrastructure at Swinburne University and has over 20 years experience in product engineering and project management in the automotive industry.

Monster Tajima 1 Megawatt electric race car
Being developed in conjunction with Rimac Automobili, this race car has been built for the Pikes Peak International Hill Climb.

CHAdeMO project update
Charge adapter finished to enable non J1772 fitted cars to charge from public charging stations.
