ATA Melbourne EV branch meeting - Melbourne EV Expo update and International developments

  • 2015-02-25 08:00:00
  • 397 Burwood road, Hawthorn VIC 3122

When: Wednesday 25 February 2015, 7:00pm

Where: Swinburne University, Hawthorn campus, Burwood road, Hawthorn, Room BA403. The BA build can be found on the Hawthorn campus map at grid reference G5, entrance is via the Atrium.

Please not the change of building: Due to room availability this months meeting will be held in room BA403.

This months meeting Paul Paton will be talking about our plans for this years Melbourne EV Expo along with our past expos and other expos from overseas.

There will be videos from the Electric Vehicle Conversion Convention in Missouri USA and Elektromobilitat in Zurich Switzerland.
