ATA Melbourne EV Branch - site visit - Renault EV information night

  • 2018-08-22 09:00:00

When: Wednesday 22 August 2018, 7:00pm
Where: Details on RSVP.

Presentation: Inspection of Renault Zoe and Kangoo electric vehicles 

There will be no monthly meeting this month as we have been invited by the AEVA to attend a "Renault Evening". This is a special event at a local Renault dealer, who will have on display models of their Zoe, Kango and possibly Twizy electric vehicles. There will a presentation about the vehicles after the viewing.

Places are strictly limited to 20 ATA members wishing to attend should RSVP by 18 August to doug@ata.org.au. Places will be given to those who reply first. If there is sufficient interest we will endeavour to organise another event later in the year. Those missing out now will be given first option at that time.

Successful members will be emailed the event details.

We hope to see you at the meeting.

Regards,
Rick Molloy
