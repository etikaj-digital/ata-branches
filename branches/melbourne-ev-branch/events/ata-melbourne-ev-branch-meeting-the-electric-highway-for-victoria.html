ATA Melbourne EV branch meeting - The Electric Highway for Victoria

  • 2017-04-26 09:00:00
  • 50 Park St, Hawthorn, VIC

When: Wednesday 26 April 2017, 7:00pm

Where: TD Building, Swinburne University, Hawthorn Campus, Room TD121 Note the change of building. If you are unfamiliar with this location view or download this Hawthorn campus map. There is parking in Park St, William St, and on the Glenferrie Rd side of the building. If travelling by train then take the same exit from the station, but continue along the walkway until you reach John St. Turn left and walk up towards Park St and the TD building is on your left.

Presentation:

Daryl Budgeon will be discussing a proposed plan for an electric highway for the State of Victoria. This paper proposed placement of Level 1, 2, 3 chargers to cover major highways and populations centres.

Daryl is the Secretary of the Victorian Branch of the Australian Electric Vehicle Association (AEVA). He has a degree in Transport from RMIT. He works in logistics and transport with past experience in Automotive manufacturing.
