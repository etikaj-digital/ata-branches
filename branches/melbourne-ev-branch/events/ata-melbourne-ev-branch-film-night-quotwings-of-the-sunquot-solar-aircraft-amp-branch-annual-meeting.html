ATA Melbourne EV branch - Film night \&quot;Wings of the Sun\&quot; - Solar Aircraft &amp; Branch annual meeting

  • 2015-11-25 08:00:00
  • 397 Burwood road, Hawthorn VIC 3122

When: Wednesday 25 November 2015, 7:00pm

Where: Room EN615, Swinburne University, Hawthorn campus, Burwood road, Hawthorn. If you have trouble locating the Engineering Building (EN) then you look at this Hawthorn campus map.

Presentation: "Wings of the Sun"

Come and see the screening of the fascinating documentary "The Wings of the Sun" which shows the construction and testing of the record-breaking ‘Solar Impulse’ solar-powered aircraft that flew across America in 2013. This is in support of the current attempt by pilots André Borschberg and Bertrand Piccard to fly its successor, ‘Solar Impulse 2’ and become the first to fly a solar-powered aircraft around the world. Their goal is to help spread the message of sustainable design.

ATA Melbourne EV Branch Annual Meeting
Short meeting before film to appoint position holders and committee of the branch.

Please note that the annual meeting component of this months meeting will be broadcast and recorded to YouTube but the film will not; you will have to be there in person to see it.
