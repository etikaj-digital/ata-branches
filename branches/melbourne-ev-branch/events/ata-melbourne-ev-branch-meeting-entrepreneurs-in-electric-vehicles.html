ATA Melbourne EV Branch meeting - Entrepreneurs in Electric vehicles

  • 2015-09-30 09:00:00
  • 397 Burwood road, Hawthorn VIC 3122

When: Wednesday 30 September 2015, 7:00pm
Where: Swinburne University, Hawthorn campus, Burwood road, Hawthorn, Room EN615. If you have trouble locating the Engineering Building (EN) then you look at this Hawthorn campus map.

This month we have four people presenting business ideas related to the area of electric vehicles.

  1. Barry Nguyen of EVX
  2. Rod Hendricks of SolarEV
  3. Ali Bahrman of Flash Rescue
  4. George Li of BajaBoard

Each will cover:

  1. A basic description of their technology
  2. How it improves some aspect of EV use
  3. The current business plan:
    • Who will buy it
    • How it will be sold to the user
    • How it will be promoted
  4. Issues or challenges that they would like your advice on
  5. The funding that they think is needed to take the technology to market

Please share the event with anyone you know interested in the EV business space.
