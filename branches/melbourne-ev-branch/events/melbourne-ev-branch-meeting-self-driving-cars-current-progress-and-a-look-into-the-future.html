Melbourne EV branch meeting - Self Driving Cars: Current progress and a look into the future

  • 2016-02-24 08:00:00
  • 397 Burwood road, Hawthorn VIC 3122

When: Wednesday 24 February 2016, 7:00pm

Where: Room EN615, Swinburne University, Hawthorn campus, Burwood road, Hawthorn. If you have trouble locating the Engineering Building (EN) then you look at this Hawthorn campus map.

Presentation: 
To start the year off our branch convener Paul Paton will be covering self driving cars, both current progress and what the future will bring including:

  • Current cars: driver assist, lane following and collision avoidance.
  • Current research: Google autonomous vehicle and others around the world.
  • Technology: LIDAR, precision GPS, computer vision, NVIDIA artificial intelligence.
  • Safety records and government regulation.
  • Formula E: RoboRace 300km/h autonomous race cars. Technology supplied by Kinetik.
  • Future opportunities and applications.
