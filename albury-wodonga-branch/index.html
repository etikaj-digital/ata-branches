Albury-Wodonga Branch

  • Overview
  • Events

The Abury-Wodonga ‘Border branch’ had a small start-up meeting of local ATA members on Wednesday 6 April 2016. Once we have sufficient local ATA members able to be on the committee then we plan to meet at the new Sustainable Activity Centre run by the amazing Claire.

We are now looking to form the local committee to begin planning public activities. If you are an ATA member and are passionate about seeing sustainability education and events in the Albury-Wodonga region then please contact Doug via email doug@ata.org.au

Interim branch Convenor: Phil Day
