ATA Albury-Wodonga \&#039;Border Branch\&#039; - start up meeting

  • 2016-04-06 09:00:00
  • Gateway Village Recreation Centre, Lincoln Causeway, Wodonga, VIC

When: Wednesday 6 April 2016, 7:00 pm

Where: ‘Sustainable Activity Centre’, Gateway Village, Lincoln Causeway, Wodonga. Click here for a map.

Come along to meet other local ATA members and to hear about renewed plans for a local ATA branch. We’ll be meeting in the new Sustainable Activity Centre. There are lots of exciting ideas for activities, but we want to hear from you too. We\'re looking for the local ATA members to show their interest in meeting together locally.

We look forward to seeing you there.

Phil Day, Heinz Menzel and Lloyd Charter
